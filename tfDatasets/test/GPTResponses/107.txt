There are several countries that have school systems somewhat similar to Northern Ireland:- Scotland: Like Northern Ireland, Scotland has a separate education system from the rest of the UK. It also has a similar structure of primary and secondary education.- Wales: While Wales is also part of the UK, its education system has its own curriculum and assessments. There is also a focus on promoting the Welsh language.- New Zealand: New Zealand has a similar system of primary and secondary education as well as a focus on student well-being and holistic learning.- Australia: The Australian education system also has a similar structure to Northern Ireland, with primary and secondary schools. There is also a focus on standards and assessments.- Canada: In Canada, the provinces and territories have their own education systems. Some, like Ontario, have a similar structure to Northern Ireland with primary and secondary schools. There is also a focus on equity and inclusivity in education.
