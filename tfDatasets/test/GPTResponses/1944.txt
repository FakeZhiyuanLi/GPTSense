There were several gods associated with plague and disease in various mythologies:1. Apollo - Greek god of medicine, prophecy, and plague2. Nergal - Babylonian god of war and the underworld, also associated with plague3. Sekhmet - Egyptian goddess of war and medicine, also known for her ability to cause and prevent plagues4. Shiva - Hindu god of destruction and transformation, also often associated with disease and epidemics5. Sitala - Hindu goddess of smallpox and other infectious diseases
