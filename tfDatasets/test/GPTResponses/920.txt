They also argued that the war was being used as an excuse to suppress dissent at home and to promote nationalist fervor among the masses, thereby distracting them from the real issues of class struggle and inequality. Many leftists in the West therefore opposed the war and advocated for peace and solidarity among workers and oppressed peoples around the world.
