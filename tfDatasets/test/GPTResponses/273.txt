Literary devices are employed to enhance the effectiveness and impact of a writer's message, to engage and capture the interest of the reader, and to convey complex ideas and emotions in a powerful way. Literary devices also add depth, richness, and beauty to a piece of writing by creating layers of meaning and nuance that can be appreciated and interpreted in different ways. By using literary devices, writers can create vivid and memorable images, highlight important themes, and create a distinctive style that sets their work apart from others. Overall, literary devices help writers achieve their intended purposes by creating a more engaging and meaningful reading experience for their audience.
