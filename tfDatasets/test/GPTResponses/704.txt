The Greeks established their trading colonies in various locations throughout the Mediterranean and Black Sea regions. Some of the most notable locations include:1. Massalia (modern-day Marseille) in southern France2. Syracuse in Sicily, Italy3. Cyrene in modern-day Libya4. Naucratis in Egypt5. Byzantium (modern-day Istanbul) in Turkey6. Odessa in Ukraine7. Panticapaeum in Crimea, Russia8. Apollonia in modern-day Albania9. Olbia in present-day Ukraine10. Emporion in northeastern Spain.
