The war had a significant impact on France's economy. Here are some of the ways:1. High military expenditure: France spent a vast amount of money on the military during the war.2. Decline in production: Many of France's industries were destroyed during the war, which led to a decline in production.3. Damage to infrastructure: The war also caused significant damage to France's infrastructure, including roads, bridges, and buildings.4. Inflation: The cost of living increased rapidly during the war, which led to inflation.5. Borrowing and debt: To fund the war effort, France had to borrow heavily from other countries, which left them with significant debt.Overall, the war left France in a precarious financial situation, which would take several years to recover from.
