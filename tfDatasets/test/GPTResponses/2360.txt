Lethargy can be caused by a variety of factors, including lack of sleep, poor nutrition, stress, depression, and certain medical conditions such as hypothyroidism and anemia.Copper deficiency can be caused by a lack of dietary copper, malabsorption due to gastrointestinal disorders, excess zinc intake, and rare genetic disorders such as Menkes disease. Symptoms of copper deficiency include anemia, neurological abnormalities, and low white blood cell count.
