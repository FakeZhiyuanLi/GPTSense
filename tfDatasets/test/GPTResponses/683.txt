Many items have replaced previously made stone items over time, including:1. Tools: Metal tools eventually replaced stone tools for their durability and strength.2. Building materials: Bricks, concrete, and steel replaced stone as primary building materials due to their availability and ease of use.3. Cooking vessels: Metal pots and pans replaced stone ones due to their ability to conduct heat more evenly.4. Writing materials: Paper and ink replaced stone tablets and chisels for writing.5. Weapons: Guns and other modern weapons replaced stone weapons due to technological advancements.6. Jewelry: Metal and synthetic materials replaced stone jewelry for their design and durability.
