?Ottoman cuisine is in reference to the food eaten in the Ottoman Empire, which was a large and influential empire that existed from the 14th to the early 20th century. The empire covered a large part of Southeast Europe, Western Asia, and North Africa, and the cuisine was influenced by various cultures and traditions. The Ottoman cuisine is known for its rich flavors, use of spices, and diverse dishes that have been passed down through generations. Today, it is considered a fusion of Turkish, Armenian, Syrian, Lebanese, Greek, and Balkan cuisines.
