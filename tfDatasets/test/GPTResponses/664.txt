The first known archaeoastronomy found in Nabta Playa includes:1. An alignment of megaliths marking the summer solstice sunrise.2. A circle of megaliths indicating a lunar calendrical cycle.3. Megaliths marking the alignment of Venus with the sun.4. A stone monument pointing to the star Sirius, which was used for tracking the seasons.5. A complex of stone circles aligned with various astronomical phenomena, including the summer and winter solstices and the equinoxes.6. A system of channels and basins that were used to align the movement of the stars with the movement of the sun and the moon.
