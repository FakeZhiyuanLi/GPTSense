Several leaders and figures throughout Indian history have emphasized the importance of education and made it a priority in the country. Here are a few notable examples:1. Rabindranath Tagore - a poet, philosopher, and scholar who founded the Visva-Bharati University in 1921 with an emphasis on education that combined tradition and modernity.2. Mahatma Gandhi - a leader of the Indian independence movement who believed in the power of education to promote social and economic equality. He advocated for widespread access to basic education and established several educational institutions himself.3. Jawaharlal Nehru - India's first Prime Minister who emphasized the importance of education in building a democratic and equitable society. He helped establish the All India Institute of Medical Sciences and the Indian Institutes of Technology, among other institutions.4. Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam - a renowned scientist and former President of India who often spoke about the need for educational reform and investment to promote India's progress.Overall, many leaders and thinkers in India have recognized the critical role of education in shaping the country's future and have worked to make it a priority.
