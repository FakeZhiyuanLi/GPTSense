Electric shock: Return currents flowing through water or metal structures can cause electric shocks to people or animals in contact with them.2. Corrosion: Return currents can cause corrosion of metal structures and pipes in contact with the water. This can weaken the structure and cause it to fail over time.3. Interference: Return currents can interfere with electronic devices and communication systems, causing them to malfunction or fail.4. Erosion: Return currents can also cause erosion of the surrounding environment, which can lead to land erosion and damage to nearby infrastructure.5.
