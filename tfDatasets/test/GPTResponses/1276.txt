The new reversible-plug connector for USB devices, known as USB Type-C, was defined by a series of technical specifications outlined by the USB Implementers Forum (USB-IF). These specifications include:1) Small size and slim design, enabling it to fit in thinner devices2) Reversible plug orientation, allowing for easy insertion and compatibility with both sides3) Support for faster data transfer rates (up to 10 Gbps)4) Ability to carry higher power loads for charging larger devices5) Support for various protocols, such as DisplayPort and HDMI, for video and audio transmission through the same connector.These features make USB Type-C a versatile and convenient solution for a wide range of devices, including smartphones, laptops, and other peripherals.
