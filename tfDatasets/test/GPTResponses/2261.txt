Foods that are high in purines can cause an increase in uric acid levels. Examples include:- Organ meats (liver, kidney, etc.)- Red meat (beef, lamb, pork, etc.)- Seafood (anchovies, sardines, mussels, scallops, etc.)- Beer and other alcoholic beverages- Some vegetables (asparagus, mushrooms, spinach, cauliflower, etc.)- High-fructose corn syrup and foods/drinks containing it.
