The government of Cyprus has invested in several projects to help supply water for the country, including:1. Dams: Numerous dams have been constructed across the country to collect rainwater and provide freshwater storage.2. Desalination Plants: Several desalination plants have been constructed that convert seawater into potable water.3. Water-saving technologies: The government has promoted water-saving technologies such as drip irrigation, which is more efficient than traditional irrigation systems.4. Water conservation campaigns: The government has launched campaigns to encourage residents to conserve water by reducing usage, repairing leaks, and using water responsibly.5. Water Recycling: The government has invested in water recycling and reuse programs, allowing treated wastewater to be used for irrigation, flushing toilets, washing cars, and other non-drinking purposes.
