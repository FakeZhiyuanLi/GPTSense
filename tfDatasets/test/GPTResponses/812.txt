There were several Greek historians who wrote about natural occurrences, including:1) Herodotus - he wrote about the flooding of the Nile and the volcanic eruption of Thera.2) Aristotle - he studied and wrote extensively about natural phenomena such as earthquakes, weather patterns, and astronomy.3) Pliny the Elder - he wrote about the eruption of Mount Vesuvius and the destruction of Pompeii.4) Thucydides - he wrote about the outbreak of the plague in Athens during the Peloponnesian War.5) Pausanias - he wrote about natural wonders such as hot springs, waterfalls, and caves throughout Greece.
