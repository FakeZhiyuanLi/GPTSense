Green paints and dyes can be made from a variety of natural and synthetic sources. Some common natural sources include plant-based materials such as:- Spinach- Chlorophyll- Indigo- Turmeric- Spirulina- Matcha powderSynthetic sources may involve chemical compounds such as:- Chromium oxide- Copper phthalocyanine- Zinc oxide- Cobalt greenThe exact composition of green paints and dyes will depend on the desired shade of green and the specific application.
