- To the woman who touched his cloak in the crowd (Mark 5:25-34): Jesus said, "Daughter, your faith has healed you. Go in peace and be freed from your suffering."- To the Samaritan woman at the well (John 4:1-30): Jesus engaged in a long conversation with her, ultimately revealing that he was the Messiah.In all these instances, Jesus treated the women with dignity and respect, showing them compassion and often healing them spiritually or physically.
