The component schools of KU Medical Center offer the following:1. School of Medicine: Offers degrees in Medicine, Biochemistry and Molecular Biology, and Neuroscience. Also, provides medical training through its affiliated hospitals and clinics.2. School of Health Professions: Offers degrees in several healthcare fields such as Physical Therapy, Occupational Therapy, Respiratory Therapy, Clinical Laboratory Science, Health Information Management, etc.3. School of Nursing: Offers degrees in Nursing and Nursing Science. Also, provides nursing education and training through its affiliated hospitals and clinics.4. Graduate Studies: Offer several research-oriented programs such as Pharmacology, Bioinformatics, Molecular Medicine, Biomedical Sciences, etc. that lead to Master's and Doctoral degrees.Overall, the KU Medical Center and its component schools provide a comprehensive range of healthcare education and training opportunities to students, clinicians, and researchers.
