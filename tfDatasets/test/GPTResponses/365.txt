J.B.S. Haldane was a scientist who was influenced by Marxism in the United Kingdom. He was a geneticist, evolutionary biologist, and a prolific writer, and was known for his Marxist views and activism. Haldane was a member of the Communist Party of Great Britain and used his scientific work to promote socialist ideas. He was a vocal critic of the government's handling of science and technology, and believed that science should be used to benefit the working class.
