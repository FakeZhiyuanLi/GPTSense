Tourism has recently become a growing industry in Tibet. The region attracts a large number of tourists due to its stunning natural beauty, ancient Buddhist culture, and unique traditions. The development of infrastructure and the ease of obtaining tourist permits have made it easier for travelers to visit the region. The tourism industry is providing significant economic benefits to Tibet, generating employment opportunities and contributing to the local economy. However, there are concerns about the impact of mass tourism on the region's fragile environment and cultural heritage.
