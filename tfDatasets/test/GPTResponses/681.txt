Avicenna is best known for his influential works in philosophy, medicine, and logic. Some of his most well-known writings include:1. The Canon of Medicine - a medical encyclopedia that became a standard text in European universities for centuries2. The Book of Healing - a philosophical and scientific encyclopedia covering topics such as physics, metaphysics, and psychology3. The Metaphysics of The Healing - a philosophical work exploring the nature of existence and reality4. The Deliverance - a philosophical work discussing the concept of knowledge and certainty5. The Drowning - a spiritual allegory exploring the nature of the soul and its journey towards divine knowledge.All of these works are widely attributed to Avicenna and have had a significant impact on Western philosophy and medicine.
