In the preamble of the Law of Treaties, several sources of international law are mentioned. These include:1. The principles of the United Nations Charter, which include the promotion of peace and security, respect for human rights, and the principle of self-determination.2. The principles of international law recognized in the Charter of the Nuremberg Tribunal and in the judgments of the International Military Tribunal, which include the prohibition of aggression, war crimes, and crimes against humanity.3. The Universal Declaration of Human Rights, which sets out basic rights and freedoms that all individuals are entitled to.4. The principles of customary international law, which are established through the consistent and widespread practice of states.5. The decisions and opinions of international courts and tribunals, which contribute to the development and interpretation of international law.
