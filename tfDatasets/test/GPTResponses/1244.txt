There are two power variants in USB 3.0 ports:1. Standard Power (SP) - This provides up to 900mA of power at 5 volts (4.5 watts). This is the most commonly used variant and is suitable for most devices that use USB ports.2. Charging Downstream Port (CDP) - This provides up to 1500mA of power at 5 volts (7.5 watts). This variant is designed specifically for charging devices such as smartphones, tablets, and other portable devices. CDP ports can also provide power even when the connected device is in use or when the computer is in sleep mode.
