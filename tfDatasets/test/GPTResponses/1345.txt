The Muslim concept of Tawhid refers to the belief in the oneness of God (Allah). It is considered the most fundamental belief in Islam, and it acknowledges that Allah is the only god and that there is no other god but Him.The concept of Tawhid implies that Allah is the sole creator, sustainer, and ruler of the universe. It also stresses that Allah has no partners, and that no other being or thing is worthy of worship or adoration except Him.In Islam, the concept of Tawhid is considered the cornerstone of faith, and it serves as a unifying principle that brings together all Muslims, regardless of their race or ethnicity. It is also considered a way of life that governs every aspect of a Muslim's existence, from daily prayers to acts of charity and kindness.Therefore, the meaning behind the Muslim concept of Tawhid is the belief in the unity and oneness of God, and this belief is considered to be at the heart of the Islamic faith.
