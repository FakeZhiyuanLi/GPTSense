Coral Way is a major road in Miami, Florida that connects the neighborhoods of Coral Gables, Coconut Grove, and Brickell. It runs east-west, stretching from Douglas Road in Coral Gables to Southwest 157th Avenue in Westchester. Coral Way also has a historic district known as "The Miracle Mile" that is located in the heart of Coral Gables, featuring a variety of shops, restaurants, and cultural landmarks.
