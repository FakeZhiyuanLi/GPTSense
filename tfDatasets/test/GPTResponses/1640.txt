Jon Savage is a British writer and journalist who has focused on music, youth culture, and social history. He started contributing articles to punk fanzines in the 1970s, including "Sniffin’ Glue" and "London’s Outrage." In the 1980s, he wrote for "Melody Maker," "Sounds," and "The Face." He has since written several books, including "England’s Dreaming: Sex Pistols and Punk Rock," "Teenage: The Creation of Youth, 1875-1945," and "1966: The Year the Decade Exploded." He continues to write about music, culture, and politics for various publications.
