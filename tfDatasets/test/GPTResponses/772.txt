A platform may be used for various reasons, but some common reasons are:1. To provide a stable and elevated surface for people to stand on and perform tasks or activities.2. To enable a larger group of people to see or hear what is happening on the platform, such as during a concert or speech.3. To create a space for displays, exhibitions or presentations to showcase products, ideas or achievements.4. To support heavy equipment or machinery, such as a construction crane or drilling rig.5. To connect different areas, such as across a gap or over water.6. To increase safety when working at height, such as on a building site or in a warehouse.7. To create a level surface for transportation, such as on a train or subway platform.
