The two palaces that are frequent tourist destinations within Rajasthan are:1. City Palace in Jaipur: The City Palace is a complex of palaces, courtyards, gardens and buildings that was built by the Maharaja of Jaipur over the course of several centuries. It is known for its stunning architecture, intricate carvings and beautiful mirror work.2. Umaid Bhawan Palace in Jodhpur: Umaid Bhawan Palace is a magnificent palace that was built by the Maharaja of Jodhpur in the 1920s. It is known for its Art Deco style, stunning gardens and sweeping views of Jodhpur. Today, part of the palace is a hotel, while the rest is a museum.
