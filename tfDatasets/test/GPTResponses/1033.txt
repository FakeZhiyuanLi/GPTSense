Confucianism emphasizes virtuous leadership in East Asian philosophy. The teachings of Confucius emphasize the importance of self-cultivation, moral character, and ethical behavior, which are seen as necessary prerequisites for effective and just leadership. Confucianism also emphasizes the value of education and emphasizes the importance of leading by example, rather than relying solely on authority and repression. Overall, Confucianism sees virtuous leadership as a key element in promoting social harmony and prosperity.
