There are many words that are disyllabic and usually written with two characters in various languages. Here are some examples in English and Chinese:English:- Happy- Hello- Apple- Human- Water- FunnyChinese:- 人民 (Rénmín) = People- 中国 (Zhōngguó) = China- 飞机 (Fēijī) = Airplane- 学校 (Xuéxiào) = School- 爱情 (Àiqíng) = Love- 电话 (Diànhuà) = Telephone
