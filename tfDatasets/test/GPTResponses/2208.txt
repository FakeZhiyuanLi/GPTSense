The two main educational hubs of Rajasthan are:1. Jaipur - It is home to several prestigious institutions such as the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Malaviya National Institute of Technology (MNIT), National Law University (NLU), Rajasthan University, and many more.2. Kota - Known as the coaching capital of India, Kota is famous for its coaching institutes that prepare students for competitive exams such as IIT-JEE, NEET, AIIMS, etc. It also has several renowned schools, colleges, and universities.
