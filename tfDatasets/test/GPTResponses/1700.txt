Public Image Ltd (PiL) is a British post-punk band formed in 1978 by singer John Lydon (previously known as Johnny Rotten of the Sex Pistols). The band was known for their experimental approach to music that incorporated elements of dub, funk, and world music. They were also known for their emphasis on individuality and creative expression, often eschewing traditional rock and punk tropes. Overall, Public Image Ltd can be classified as an avant-garde post-punk group.
