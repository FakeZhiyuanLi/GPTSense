After finishing comprehensive school (secondary education) in Gibraltar, students have multiple options for further education:1. Gibraltar College: Students can continue their education at the Gibraltar College, which offers a range of vocational and academic courses.2. University of Gibraltar: The University of Gibraltar offers undergraduate and postgraduate degree programs in various fields including Business, Education, and Marine Science.3. Study abroad: Students can also choose to study abroad in countries like the UK, Spain, or other European countries.4. Apprenticeships: Students can enter into apprenticeships in various trades and gain real-world work experience while learning on the job.5. Employment: Students can choose to enter the workforce directly after completing secondary education and work in various industries.Overall, students in Gibraltar have a variety of options available to them after completing secondary education to further their education or enter the workforce.
