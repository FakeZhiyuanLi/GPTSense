Spain has invaded several countries throughout its history. Some of the most notable examples include:- The Americas: Spain invaded and colonized many countries in Central and South America, including Mexico, Peru, and Argentina.- The Philippines: Spain colonized the Philippines in the 16th century and ruled it until the late 19th century.- Morocco: Spain invaded and occupied northern parts of Morocco in the early 20th century.- Equatorial Guinea: Spain colonized this African country in the late 19th century and ruled it until 1968.
