To operate properly, guide rails require the following:1. Proper alignment: The guide rails must be installed in the correct position, properly aligned with each other and the track.2. Proper attachment: The guide rails must be securely attached to the track and the support structure.3. Adequate lubrication: The guide rails must be lubricated to reduce friction and ensure smooth movement of the elevator car.4. Proper maintenance: Regular inspection and maintenance of the guide rails is essential to ensure that they remain in good condition and continue to operate properly.5. Correct tension: The guide rails must be properly tensioned to prevent excessive bouncing or wobbling of the elevator car during operation.6. Correct size: The guide rails must be the correct size to support the weight of the elevator car and its occupants.
