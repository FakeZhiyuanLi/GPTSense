Rajasthani folk songs often depict heroic deeds of valor, chivalry, and bravery. These songs are popularly known as "Kathas" or "Veer Ras" songs. They celebrate the bravery and courage of Rajput warriors, who fought against foreign invaders, defending their land and honor. The Rajasthani folk songs are often accompanied by traditional musical instruments like dholak, harmonium, sarangi, and bhapang. They are an integral part of Rajasthani culture and are performed on numerous occasions, including weddings, festivals, and cultural fairs.
