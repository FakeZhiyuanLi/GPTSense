French is taught in school for several reasons:1. International importance: French is spoken by around 220 million people worldwide and is an official language in 29 countries. It is also one of the official languages of international organizations like the United Nations, UNESCO, and the International Olympic Committee.2. Cultural significance: French is widely regarded as a language of culture, art, and literature. Many important works of literature, philosophy, and art are originally in French.3. Career advantage: The ability to speak French can open up career opportunities in various fields like diplomacy, journalism, tourism, and international business.4. Language diversity: Learning French can help students appreciate linguistic diversity and gain insights into different cultures and ways of thinking.5. Personal enrichment: Studying French can be a way for students to broaden their perspective and gain a deeper appreciation of the world around them.
