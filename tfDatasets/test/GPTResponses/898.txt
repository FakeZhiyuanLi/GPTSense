Alvah Bessie2. Herbert Biberman3. Lester Cole4. Edward Dmytryk5. Ring Lardner Jr.6. John Howard Lawson7. Albert Maltz8. Sam Ornitz9. Adrian Scott10. Dalton Trumbo
