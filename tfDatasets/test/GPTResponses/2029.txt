There are various organizations and government agencies that may provide financial assistance for the care of harmed wildlife. These may include:1. Wildlife rehabilitation centers: Many wildlife rehabilitation centers provide free or low-cost care to injured, orphaned, or sick wildlife.2. Non-profit organizations: Non-profit organizations like the World Wildlife Fund, National Wildlife Federation, and International Fund for Animal Welfare may provide financial assistance for wildlife care.3. Government agencies: Government agencies like the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service and state wildlife agencies may provide grants or other funding for wildlife rehabilitation.4. Donors and fundraising campaigns: Donors and fundraising campaigns may also provide financial assistance for wildlife care.
