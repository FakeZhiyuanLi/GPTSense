There are several things that are getting cheaper as a result of technological change. Here are a few examples:1. Electronics: With every passing year, electronics such as smartphones, laptops, and televisions are becoming cheaper due to advancements in technology and improved manufacturing processes.2. Renewable energy: The cost of renewable energy sources such as solar and wind power is decreasing due to the development of more efficient and cost-effective technologies.3. Healthcare: Technology is improving patient care and reducing costs through the use of telemedicine, electronic health records, and health monitoring devices.4. Transportation: The development of electric and hybrid vehicles is reducing the cost of transportation and making it more environmentally friendly.5. Education: Online education and e-learning platforms are making education more affordable and accessible to people around the world.
