Set phrases are phrases or sayings that are commonly used and have a fixed form and meaning. Examples of set phrases include:1. "At the end of the day"2. "In the nick of time"3. "Once in a blue moon"4. "The ball is in your court"5. "Actions speak louder than words"6. "All is fair in love and war"7. "Bite the bullet"8. "Break a leg"9. "Burning the midnight oil"10. "Every cloud has a silver lining"
