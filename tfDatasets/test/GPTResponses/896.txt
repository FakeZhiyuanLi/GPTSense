The rule of law must have the following characteristics according to legal theorists:1. Equality before the law - It means that everybody is equal before the law, and no one is above it.2. Due process - It means that the legal process must be fair, transparent, and unbiased.3. Transparency - It means that the legal process and decisions should be open to scrutiny and review.4. Consistency - It means that the law should be applied consistently and without discrimination.5. Accessibility - It means that the law should be accessible to everyone, and they should be able to seek redress through legal means.6. Predictability - It means that the law should be predictable, and people should know what to expect when they break it.7. Accountability - It means that those who enforce the law must be accountable for their actions, and there should be sufficient checks and balances in place.8. Respect for fundamental human rights - It means that the rule of law must respect fundamental human rights, such as freedom of speech, freedom of association, and freedom of religion.
