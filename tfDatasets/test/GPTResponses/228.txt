The name of the group that defeats Cody and Ken varies depending on the specific game, movie or show in which they appear. However, some possible groups that have defeated them in various contexts are:- Street Fighter Alpha 3: Team Bison (Balrog, Vega, and M. Bison)- Street Fighter II: The Animated Movie: Shadowlaw (M. Bison, Balrog, Vega, Sagat, and various Shadaloo soldiers)- Street Fighter V: Shadaloo (M. Bison, F.A.N.G, and various other Shadaloo agents) or A Shadow Falls protagonists (Ryu, Chun-Li, Nash, Ken, Rashid, Cammy, and Guile)
