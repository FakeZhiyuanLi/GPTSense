The Unihan Database lists approximately 90,000 common and rare characters used in Chinese, Japanese, and Korean scripts. However, if you are looking for a list of the 2,500 most commonly used characters in Chinese, you can refer to the HSK (Hanyu Shuiping Kaoshi) word list that is used for Chinese language proficiency testing. The HSK word list consists of about 2,500 characters and vocabulary words that are commonly used in everyday communication.
