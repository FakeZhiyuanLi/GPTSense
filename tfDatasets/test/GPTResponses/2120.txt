The Arabs introduced several things that continue to be relevant to Palermo's culture today, such as:1. Architecture: The Arab influence is prominent in Palermo's architecture, particularly in the use of arches, domes, and ornate decorations on buildings.2. Cuisine: The Arab influence on Palermo's cuisine is still evident today, particularly in the use of spices, dried fruits, and local seafood.3. Agricultural practices: The Arabs introduced new agricultural practices and crops to Palermo, such as citrus fruits, almonds, and sugar cane.4. Language: The Sicilian language includes many words of Arabic origin, particularly in the vocabulary related to agriculture and cuisine.5. Music: The Arab influence on Palermo's music is still present today, particularly in traditional folk music that includes elements of Arabic rhythms and melodies.
