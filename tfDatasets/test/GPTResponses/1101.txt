Several types of annelid fossils have been found from the mid-Ordovician period, including:1. Graptolites: These are colonial organisms that belong to the class Graptolithina, which are considered to be distant relatives of modern-day annelids.2. Polychaetes: These are segmented worms that belong to the class Polychaeta. Fossil specimens of these worms have been found from the mid-Ordovician period.3. Scolecodonts: These are the dental elements of polychaete worms, which are found as microscopic fossils. Scolecodont fossils from the mid-Ordovician period have been found in various locations around the world.
