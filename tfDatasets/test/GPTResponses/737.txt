There were several policies that contributed to the success of the Mughal dynasty, including:1. Religious tolerance: The Mughal emperors were known for their tolerance towards people of different religions, which helped them to win the support of diverse communities across their empire.2. Land revenue system: The Mughals implemented a system of revenue collection, known as the zabt system, which helped to increase the revenue of the empire and ensure the equitable distribution of resources.3. Administration: The Mughals had an efficient administrative system that helped to maintain law and order and promote economic growth throughout the empire.4. Military strength: The Mughals had a powerful army, which helped them to expand their empire and defend their borders from external threats.5. Cultural patronage: The Mughal emperors were great patrons of art, literature, and culture, which helped to create a rich and vibrant cultural heritage for the empire.
