Rajasthani folk music plays a big part in the culture of Rajasthan. It is an integral part of their social and cultural traditions and is performed on various occasions such as weddings, festivals, and religious ceremonies. The most popular musical instruments used in Rajasthani folk music are the dholak, harmonium, tabla, and sarangi. The songs are usually sung in the local dialects of Rajasthan and often tell stories of historical and mythological events. Kalbeliya and Ghoomar are two popular traditional dance forms of Rajasthan that are often accompanied by folk music.
