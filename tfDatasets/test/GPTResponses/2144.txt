Rajasthani cooking typically involves vegetarian dishes that are rich in spices and flavors. Some of the preferred foods in Rajasthani cuisine include dal baati churma, gatte ki sabzi, ker sangria, besan ki chakki, and bajre ki roti. Dairy products like ghee, milk, and yogurt are also commonly used in Rajasthani cooking. Meat dishes, such as laal maas (a spicy lamb curry), are popular in certain regions of Rajasthan.
