The fully featured USB Type-C cable contains the following cables:1. USB 3.1 Gen 2 – This cable supports data transfer speeds of up to 10Gbps and power delivery up to 100W.2. DisplayPort – This cable supports resolutions up to 8K and can output audio and video signals.3. Thunderbolt 3 – This cable supports data transfer speeds of up to 40Gbps, power delivery up to 100W, and can output audio and video signals.4. HDMI – This cable supports resolutions of up to 4K and can output audio and video signals.5. Ethernet – This cable supports a maximum data transfer speed of 10Gbps and can be used to connect to a local network.6. Audio – This cable supports audio output and input, and can be used to connect to headphones, microphones, and speakers.
