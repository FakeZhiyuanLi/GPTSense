Two other Abrahamic religions that were practiced in India before the introduction of Islam are Judaism and Christianity.Judaism has a long history in India, with the first Jewish settlement believed to have occurred in the 6th century BCE. There are still small Jewish communities present in India today, mostly in Mumbai, Kolkata, and Kochi.Christianity was first introduced to India by the Apostle Thomas in the 1st century CE. He is said to have established churches in various parts of South India. Today, Christianity is one of the major religions in India, with approximately 28 million Christians.
