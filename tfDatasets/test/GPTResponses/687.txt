The Kitab al-Shifa is a comprehensive Islamic manuscript written by the Muslim philosopher, physician and theologian, Abu Ali ibn Sina (also known as Avicenna) in the 11th century. The book covers a wide range of subjects, including philosophy, cosmology, ethics, metaphysics, psychology, and medicine. It is considered one of the foundational texts of Islamic philosophy and was widely studied and influential in both the Islamic and non-Islamic worlds.
