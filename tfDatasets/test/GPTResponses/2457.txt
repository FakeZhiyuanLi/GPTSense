Insects can sense a variety of things, including:1. Smells: Insects have highly developed olfactory senses that allow them to detect pheromones, food, and potential mates.2. Sounds: Many insects use hearing organs to listen for the sounds of prey, predators, or other insects.3. Temperature: Insects can sense changes in temperature and adjust their behavior accordingly.4. Light: Insects have complex eyes that allow them to see different wavelengths of light, including ultraviolet.5. Vibrations: Some insects have specialized organs that allow them to detect vibrations, which can help them locate prey or avoid predators.
