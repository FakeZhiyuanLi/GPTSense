Consumption of excess zinc can lead to several health problems, including:1. Nausea and vomiting2. Stomach cramps and diarrhea3. Headaches and fatigue4. Reduced immune system function5. Interference with copper absorption, leading to anemia and neurological problems6. Formation of kidney stones7. Loss of appetite and weight loss8. Increased risk of prostate cancer and other disorders of the prostate gland9. Impaired cognition and learning disabilities in children10. Skin irritation and rash.Consuming more than the recommended daily intake of zinc (11 mg for men, 8 mg for women) for an extended period can lead to these consequences.
