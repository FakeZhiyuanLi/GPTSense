The Chola Empire was established as a powerful maritime empire due to their contacts and conquests in surrounding sea areas. They had a strong navy that allowed them to control and dominate trade routes across the Indian Ocean, with their influence extending to parts of Southeast Asia and the Middle East. The Chola Empire was also known for its cultural and artistic achievements, with notable contributions in fields such as architecture, sculpture, and literature. Overall, their maritime activities and achievements established the Chola Empire as a prominent and influential civilization in ancient history.
