There were several important processes that occurred around the 12th century, including:1. The Crusades: A series of religious wars fought between Christians and Muslims in the Holy Land, starting in 1096 and lasting until the late 13th century.2. The Renaissance: A cultural and intellectual movement that began in Italy and spread throughout Europe, which emphasized the revival of classical learning and arts.3. Gothic architecture: The emergence of a new style of architecture characterized by pointed arches, ribbed vaults, and flying buttresses, which allowed for greater height and light in church buildings.4. Feudalism: The decline of feudalism as a political and social system in Europe, as centralized monarchies began to take shape.5. University system: The development of medieval universities, which laid the groundwork for modern higher education and scholarship.
