The concept of resurrection is mentioned multiple times in the book of Revelation. In chapter 20 verses 4-6, it is described that the souls of those who were beheaded for their testimony of Jesus and for the word of God will come to life and reign with Christ for a thousand years.In chapter 11 verse 12, two witnesses of God who were killed will be resurrected after three and a half days and will ascend to heaven.In chapter 14 verses 13, it is mentioned that those who die in the Lord will rest from their labors and their deeds will follow them.Overall, the book of Revelation emphasizes the belief in an afterlife and the ultimate victory of God's kingdom over evil.
