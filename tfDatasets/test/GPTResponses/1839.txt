There are several prompts that can lead to the need for formal consultation. These include:1. Complex or difficult cases: When a case involves complex issues or difficult decisions, it may be necessary to seek the input of other professionals through formal consultation.2. Ethical dilemmas: When there are ethical dilemmas involved in a case, consulting with other professionals can help ensure that the best possible decision is made.3. Challenging clients or situations: If a client is particularly challenging or if a situation is particularly difficult to manage, consultation may be necessary to ensure that the best possible outcome is achieved.4. New or unfamiliar situations: When dealing with situations or clients that are new or unfamiliar, consulting with other professionals can help ensure that the appropriate approach is taken.5. Legal concerns: When there are legal concerns involved in a case, consultation with legal professionals may be necessary to ensure compliance with the law.
