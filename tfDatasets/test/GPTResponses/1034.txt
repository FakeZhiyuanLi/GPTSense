Confucianism is the East Asian philosophy that emphasizes unwavering rule-following. Confucianism stresses the importance of social order, moral harmony, and respect for authority. The followers of Confucianism believe that adherence to strict moral principles is essential for creating a stable and harmonious society. They also emphasize the importance of education, self-cultivation, and the cultivation of virtues such as loyalty, filial piety, and righteousness.
