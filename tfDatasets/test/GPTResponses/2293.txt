Apart from traditional Cypriot music, the people of Cyprus also enjoy a variety of genres from around the world, including pop, rock, hip hop, reggae, jazz, classical, electronic, and world music. Some popular Cypriot artists include Anna Vissi, Michalis Hatzigiannis, and Ivi Adamou, while international musicians like Rihanna, Ed Sheeran, and Beyonce also have a significant following in Cyprus. Additionally, there is a growing underground music scene in Cyprus, with local bands and DJs playing alternative and experimental genres such as post-punk, psychedelic rock, and techno.
