The Ontario Rugby Football Union (ORFU) adopted several Canadian football rules in 1903, including:1. The use of the forward pass2. The touchdown being worth five points instead of four3. The conversion after a touchdown being worth one point instead of two4. The introduction of the line of scrimmage5. The prohibition of players being offside at the kickoff6. The implementation of a five-yard offside rule for players on the scrimmage line7. The adjustment of the size of the field to 110 yards long and 65 yards wide.
