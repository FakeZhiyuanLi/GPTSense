Various territories and regions eventually became a part of the Grand Duchy of Lithuania, including:- The Duchy of Samogitia- The Principality of Kiev- The Principality of Halych- The Duchy of Volhynia- The Duchy of Podolia- The Duchy of Trakai- The Principality of Polotsk- The Duchy of Livonia- The Duchy of Courland- The Duchy of Prussia.
