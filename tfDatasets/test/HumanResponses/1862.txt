he licked her hand as she slept. just the back of her hand, not the fingers or anything. he wanted to taste the brackish velvet skin. her position was fetal, but with legs scissored, as if she was jumping off a diving board to perform for a drunken crowd. she would leave as soon as she woke up, he knew it. her scattered clothes were almost set up perfectly so she could dress without breaking stride. even her kate spade sat right next to his apartment door, handles erect, waiting to be snatched with nary a cheek kiss. her voice would fade as the elevator door closed. call me she would say. call meeeee. he thinks there is a sandwich in the fridge. or a glass of water. water would be better. she is waking up.
