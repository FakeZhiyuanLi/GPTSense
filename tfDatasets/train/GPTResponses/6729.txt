People dress in masks and costumes in various settings, such as:1. Carnival celebrations like the famous Carnival of Venice, Carnival of Brazil, and Mardi Gras in New Orleans, where parades, parties, and other festivities are held.2. Halloween celebrations, where people dress up in costumes and masks to scare or entertain others.3. Comic book conventions, where fans of pop culture dress up as their favorite characters from movies, TV shows, and comic books.4. Renaissance fairs, where people dress up in medieval costumes and participate in historical reenactments.5. Dance parties like masquerade balls or Venetian balls, where guests wear elegant masks and costumes.
