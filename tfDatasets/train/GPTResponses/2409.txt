Thanksgiving?Traditionally, meals served on Thanksgiving include turkey, stuffing or dressing, mashed potatoes, gravy, cranberry sauce, sweet potatoes or yams, green beans or other vegetables, bread rolls or cornbread, and pumpkin or pecan pie for dessert. Other popular dishes might include ham, roasted vegetables, macaroni and cheese, and various types of pies or other desserts. Additionally, regional and cultural variations may result in other dishes being served, such as corn pudding, collard greens, or sweet potato pie.
