Non-competitive football matches are usually for fun, social or practice purposes. Some examples include:1. Friendly matches between two teams that are not part of any league or competition.2. Training matches between teammates or within a club.3. Charity matches or exhibition matches for fundraising or promotion.4. Youth matches that are non-competitive to develop skills and teamwork.5. Small-sided matches with modified rules, such as futsal, beach soccer or indoor soccer.
