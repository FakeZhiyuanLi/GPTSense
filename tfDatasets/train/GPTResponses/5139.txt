Gymnastics is a sport that involves performing a series of exercises which require physical strength, flexibility, agility, balance, coordination and control. These exercises include tumbling, flipping, balance beam, uneven bars, vaulting, rings and rhythmic dancing. Gymnastics serves as a foundation for other sports, such as acrobatics, diving, dance, and figure skating, and offers physical and mental benefits, including improved posture, stamina, confidence and overall well-being.
