Some of the creatures included in the kingdom Animalia are:1. Mammals (e.g. humans, dogs, cats, cows, horses, whales)2. Birds (e.g. eagles, pigeons, penguins, owls, parrots)3. Fish (e.g. salmon, tuna, shark, clownfish)4. Reptiles (e.g. snakes, lizards, turtles, crocodiles)5. Amphibians (e.g. frogs, toads, salamanders)6. Invertebrates (e.g. insects, spiders, worms, jellyfish, snails, crabs)7. Protozoa (single-celled organisms such as amoebas and paramecium)8. Annelids (e.g. earthworms, leeches)9. Cnidarians (e.g. corals, jellyfish, sea anemones)10. Echinoderms (e.g. starfish, sea urchins, sand dollars).
