The mode of travel for Antarctic tours varies depending on the type of tour and the company providing it. Expedition cruises are the most popular way to visit Antarctica, with smaller ships providing a more intimate experience. Some cruises offer kayaking and camping activities as well.Other tours may involve flying to Antarctica, either to a base station or to a special landing spot, and then exploring the continent on foot or by vehicle.Overall, the mode of travel for Antarctic tours typically involves a mix of transportation options, including boats, planes, and specialized vehicles designed for the extreme conditions of the continent.
