Two areas that saw a significant renewal after the post-war period and significantly modernized the city are:1. Tokyo, Japan - After World War II, Tokyo underwent a rapid urban renewal to rebuild the city from the large-scale damages caused by the war. The city was modernized with new infrastructure, buildings, and transportation systems, making it one of the most developed and well-planned cities in the world.2. Berlin, Germany - After the fall of the Berlin Wall in 1989, the city underwent a massive transformation with a significant urban renewal program. The city was modernized with new architectural developments, infrastructure, and public spaces, including the famous Potsdamer Platz, which now serves as a symbol of the rejuvenated city.
