State courts hear a variety of cases, including but not limited to:1. Criminal cases: These involve people accused of committing crimes such as theft, murder, assault, or drug offenses.2. Civil cases: These involve disputes between individuals or organizations, such as contract disputes, personal injury claims, or property disputes.3. Family law cases: These involve issues such as divorce, child custody, and child support.4. Probate cases: These involve the distribution of assets and property after someone's death.5. Traffic cases: These involve violations such as speeding, driving under the influence, or reckless driving.6. Small claims cases: These involve disputes of small monetary value, generally less than $10,000.7. Administrative law cases: These involve disputes with government agencies, such as disputes over zoning or licensing decisions.
