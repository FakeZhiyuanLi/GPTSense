Sanskrit was a learned language of the high classes primarily in ancient India, notably during the Vedic period (1500-500 BCE) and later during the Gupta period (320-550 CE). It was the language of the Brahmin class, and knowledge of Sanskrit was a symbol of high status and education. Sanskrit was used for religious and philosophical texts, as well as for literature and scientific works. It was also the language of the court and government during some periods.
