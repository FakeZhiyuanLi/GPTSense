Pesticides are used for various purposes, but their most common application is for controlling pests and diseases in agricultural crops. They are also used in residential areas, parks, golf courses, and other public areas to control weeds, insects, and rodents. Additionally, pesticides are used in the production of animal feed and the management of pests in animal husbandry. Some pesticides are used for storage of grains and other food products to protect them from insect infestation. Lastly, pesticides are also used in public health for controlling vectors of diseases such as mosquitoes, ticks, and fleas.
