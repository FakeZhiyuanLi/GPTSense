The Padma Awards are a set of civilian awards presented to deserving individuals of India for exceptional and distinguished service in their respective fields. These awards consist of three categories:1. Padma Vibhushan: This is the highest civilian award in India and is given for exceptional and distinguished service in any field including art, literature, education, science and technology, sports, public affairs, social work, and civil service.2. Padma Bhushan: This is the second-highest civilian award in India and is given for distinguished service of a high order in any field.3. Padma Shri: This is the third-highest civilian award in India and is given for distinguished service in any field. It includes art, literature and education, social work, medicine, sports, and public affairs, among others.
