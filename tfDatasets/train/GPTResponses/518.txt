Buddhists believe that suffering can be ended through following the Eightfold Path, which consists of:1. Right Understanding2. Right Intent3. Right Speech4. Right Action5. Right Livelihood6. Right Effort7. Right Mindfulness8. Right ConcentrationBy following these principles, Buddhists believe that they can achieve a state of enlightenment or Nirvana, which liberates them from the cycle of birth and rebirth and ultimately ends suffering. Additionally, practicing meditation and cultivating compassion for others can help alleviate suffering in this life.
