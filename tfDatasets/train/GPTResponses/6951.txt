Erector Square Complex is located in New Haven, Connecticut, in the United States. The address is 315 Peck St, New Haven, CT 06513, USA. It is a multi-use complex that houses artists' studios, offices, and galleries. The complex was originally built in 1916 as a factory for Erector Set toys and was later repurposed into a space for artists and small businesses. The gallery within the complex hosts exhibitions and events featuring local and international artists.
