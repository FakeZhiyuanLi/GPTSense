There are many individuals who founded monastic orders in their lifetime. Here are a few examples:1. St. Benedict of Nursia - Founder of the Order of Saint Benedict.2. St. Francis of Assisi - Founder of the Franciscan Order.3. St. Dominic - Founder of the Dominican Order.4. St. Ignatius of Loyola - Founder of the Society of Jesus (Jesuits).5. St. Teresa of Avila - Founder of the Discalced Carmelites.6. St. Clare of Assisi - Founder of the Poor Clares.7. St. Bruno - Founder of the Carthusian Order.8. St. John of God - Founder of the Hospitaller Order of St. John of God.9. St. Basil the Great - Founder of the Basilian Order.10. St. Vincent de Paul - Founder of the Congregation of the Mission (Vincentians).
