Himachal Pradesh experiences a wide range of climatic conditions due to its varied altitude and landscape. The state has three major climatic zones - the foothills, the mid-hills, and the high-altitude region.The foothills region has a subtropical climate with hot summers and mild winters. The temperature ranges from 15°C to 38°C, and the region receives heavy rainfall during the monsoon season (June to September).The mid-hills region has a temperate climate with cool summers and cold winters. The temperature ranges from 0°C to 30°C, and the region receives moderate rainfall throughout the year.The high-altitude region has an alpine climate with cold summers and freezing winters. The temperature ranges from -20°C to 15°C, and the region receives heavy snowfall during winters.Overall, Himachal Pradesh has a pleasant and enjoyable climate throughout the year. The best time to visit the state is during the summer months (April to June) and the autumn season (September to November).
