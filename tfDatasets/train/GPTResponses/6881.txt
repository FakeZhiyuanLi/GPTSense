The three offshoots of Cubism formed in France were:1. Analytic Cubism - characterized by the fragmentation of forms and a monochromatic color scheme. Artists involved in this movement include Pablo Picasso and Georges Braque.2. Synthetic Cubism - characterized by the use of collage elements and a brighter color palette. Artists involved in this movement include Juan Gris and Robert Delaunay.3. Orphism - a form of Cubism that emphasized the use of vibrant colors and abstract forms.
