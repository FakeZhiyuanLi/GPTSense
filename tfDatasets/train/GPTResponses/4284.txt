Racial and Ethnic Diversity: Houston is one of the most diverse cities in the United States, with a population that includes significant numbers of Hispanic/Latino, African American, Asian and White residents.2. Cultural Diversity: Houston offers a vibrant mix of cultures, with music, arts, food, and festivals that showcase a variety of traditions and styles from around the world.5.
