1. Exploration: This aspect refers to the process of exploring different aspects of oneself, such as values, beliefs, interests, and personality traits. This exploration may involve trying out different roles or behaviors, seeking new experiences, or reflecting on past experiences to gain a better understanding of oneself.2. Commitment: Once an individual has explored different aspects of their identity, they may then begin to make commitments to certain beliefs, values, interests, or roles. This commitment involves a sense of ownership and acceptance of their identity, as well as a willingness to stand by their choices even in the face of challenges or criticism.
