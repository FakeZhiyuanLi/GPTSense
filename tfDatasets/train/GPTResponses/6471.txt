Sexual attraction refers to the degree of interest or desire one has towards another person's physical and emotional characteristics. It can be based on various factors, such as physical appearance, personality, and overall compatibility.Sexual behavior refers to the actions and activities that people engage in to express their sexual attraction or desire for another person. These can range from intimate physical contact (such as kissing or sexual intercourse) to more subtle forms of flirting or communication.Sexual identity is a person's sense of who they are, both in terms of their sexual orientation and their gender identity. It involves a complex interplay between biological, psychological, and sociocultural factors, and can shape a person's behavior and relationships. Sexual identity can be fluid and can change over time, or remain stable throughout a person's life.
