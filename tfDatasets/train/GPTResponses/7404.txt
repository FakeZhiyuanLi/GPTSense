Many non-profit organizations and charities donate discarded western clothing to people in poor places. Some examples include:1. Goodwill Industries International: They collect and sell donated clothing and goods to support job training and employment services for people with disabilities and those facing challenges to finding employment.2. World Vision: They provide clothing and other goods to those living in poverty around the world, including disaster-stricken areas.3. Dress for Success: They provide professional clothing and career development services to help women achieve economic independence.4. Salvation Army: They collect and distribute clothing and goods to those in need, including homeless individuals and families experiencing financial hardship.5. Oxfam: They collect and donate clothing and goods to support their poverty reduction and disaster relief programs around the world.
