Aeneas was trying to accomplish multiple objectives in the Aeneid.Firstly, he was trying to fulfill his destiny or fate as outlined by the gods, which was to found a new city that will become the home of the Roman people.Secondly, he was trying to avenge his Trojan comrades who were killed in the Trojan War by the Greeks.Thirdly, he was trying to establish his leadership among his followers and convince them to continue their journey even when they faced numerous challenges and obstacles.Lastly, he was trying to establish a connection between Trojan and Roman history and establish his divine ancestry as the son of the goddess Venus, which would legitimize his claim to leadership.
