There were several notable resignations in 1954, including:1) Secretary of State John Foster Dulles resigned on April 15, 1954, due to his failing health.2) Joseph McCarthy's chief legal counsel, Roy Cohn, resigned in July 1954 after a public falling-out with McCarthy.3) British Prime Minister Winston Churchill announced his resignation on April 5, 1954, citing his advanced age and declining health.4) Mexican President Adolfo Ruiz Cortines resigned on November 30, 1954, due to health issues and concerns about his government's economic policies.
