There have been numerous individuals and organizations who have helped with free elections throughout history. Some of the most notable include:1. The United Nations: The UN has worked to promote free and fair elections around the world through various election observation missions and initiatives.2. Non-governmental organizations (NGOs): There are many organizations that work to support democratic processes and free elections, such as the Carter Center, National Democratic Institute, and the International Foundation for Electoral Systems (IFES).3. Governments: Many governments provide financial and technical support to developing countries to help them establish and conduct free and fair elections.4. Civil society groups: Local groups such as human rights organizations, trade unions, and women's rights groups often play an important role in election monitoring and advocacy.5. Individuals: Journalists, lawyers, and activists often volunteer their time and expertise to ensure that elections are conducted in an open and transparent manner.
