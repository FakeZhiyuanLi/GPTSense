The name of the god of death can vary depending on the culture and mythology. Here are a few examples:- In Greek mythology, the god of death is Hades.- In Norse mythology, the god of death is Hel.- In Hindu mythology, the god of death is Yama.- In Egyptian mythology, the god of death is Osiris or Anubis.- In Aztec mythology, the god of death is Mictlantecuhtli.
