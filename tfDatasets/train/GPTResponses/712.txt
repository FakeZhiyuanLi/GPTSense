The Four Noble Truths in Buddhism explain the nature of suffering and how to overcome it. They teach that:1. Suffering is inherent in human existence.2. Suffering arises from craving and attachment.3. Suffering can be overcome by letting go of craving and attachment.4. The path to the cessation of suffering is the Eightfold Path, which includes right understanding, intention, speech, conduct, livelihood, effort, mindfulness, and concentration.
