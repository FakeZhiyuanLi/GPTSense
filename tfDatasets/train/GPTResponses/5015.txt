Pandit Gopi Chand Narang was one of the great Sanskrit scholars of Himachal Pradesh. He was born in the village of Dina in the district of Kangra in 1938. He received his education in Sanskrit from the renowned scholar, Haribhadra Suri, and went on to become a distinguished teacher and researcher in the field of Sanskrit. He has authored several books and research papers on Sanskrit literature and is considered as one of the foremost authorities on the subject. He has received numerous awards and honors for his contributions to Sanskrit scholarship.
