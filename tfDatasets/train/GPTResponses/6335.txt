There are multiple organizations working towards the revival of Sanskrit. Some of them are:1. Sanskrit Bharati: It is a non-profit organization that aims to promote the use of Sanskrit language in everyday life. They conduct various training programmes, workshops, and courses for people to learn Sanskrit.2. Rashtriya Sanskrit Sansthan: This is an autonomous organization established by the Indian government to develop and promote Sanskrit studies. They offer various courses like Shastri (B.A.), Acharya (M.A.) and Ph.D. in Sanskrit.3. Samskrita Bharati: It is a non-profit organization that aims to revive Sanskrit as a spoken language. They organize various camps and workshops for people to learn and speak Sanskrit fluently.4. International Association of Sanskrit Studies: It is a global organization that aims to promote the study of Sanskrit in all its aspects. They organize conferences, seminars, and workshops to enhance the knowledge and understanding of Sanskrit.These organizations are actively working towards the revival and promotion of the Sanskrit language.
