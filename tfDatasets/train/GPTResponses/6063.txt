1. The Mahabharata - one of the two major Sanskrit epic poems of ancient India, the Mahabharata is an epic narrative of the Kurukshetra War and the fates of the Kaurava and the Pandava princes.2. The Ramayana - the other major Sanskrit epic poem, the Ramayana tells the story of Rama, an incarnation of the god Vishnu, and his wife Sita, and their journey to rescue her after she is kidnapped by the demon king Ravana.
