Britain intervened in many power struggles throughout history, but here are a few notable examples:1. The Scramble for Africa (1884-1885) - Britain, along with other European powers, scrambled for control of Africa, leading to the colonization and exploitation of the continent.2. The Boxer Rebellion (1899-1901) - Britain was involved in the suppression of the Boxer Rebellion in China, which was a violent anti-foreigner and anti-Christian movement.3. World War I (1914-1918) - Britain intervened in the global conflict to defend its allies and to protect its own interests in the region.4. The Falklands War (1982) - Britain went to war with Argentina to defend its claim to the Falkland Islands.5. The Gulf War (1990-1991) - Britain was part of the international coalition that intervened in the conflict between Iraq and Kuwait.
