The ISO/IEC High Standard Document states that the decompressed output produced from a given MP3 file will be the same within the following standards:1. Sampling rate of 32, 44.1, or 48 kHz2. Bit rate of 32, 40, 48, 56, 64, 80, 96, 112, 128, 160, 192, 224, 256, 320, or variable bit rate (VBR)3. Stereo or mono channel mode4. Joint stereo or dual channel mode (for bit rates above 128 kbps)
