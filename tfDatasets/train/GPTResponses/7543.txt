Due to the presence of coral reefs, popular activities in Biscayne Bay include snorkeling, diving, kayaking, boating, fishing, and eco-tours. The coral reefs in the bay provide excellent opportunities for recreational activities and for viewing marine wildlife such as tropical fish, crustaceans, sea turtles, and marine mammals, including dolphins and manatees. Additionally, visitors can enjoy beachcombing, picnicking, and exploring the various islands and coves within the bay.
