There are several factors that aggravate obesity to continue the vicious cycle, including:1. Sedentary lifestyle: Lack of physical activity slows down metabolism and increases weight gain, leading to obesity.2. Unhealthy eating habits: Consumption of high-calorie foods, fast foods, sugary drinks, and processed foods contribute to obesity.3. Genetics: People with a family history of obesity are more likely to develop obesity and struggle to lose weight.4. Hormonal imbalances: Hormones like insulin, leptin, and ghrelin play a critical role in regulating appetite and metabolism, and imbalances can lead to obesity.5. Mental health issues: Mental health issues like stress, anxiety, and depression can lead to overeating and contribute to obesity.
