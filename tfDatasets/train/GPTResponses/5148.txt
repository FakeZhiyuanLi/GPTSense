The environmental protocol's goal is to manage the use of natural resources sustainably and prevent the harmful impact of human activities on the environment. This includes reducing air, water, and soil pollution, controlling greenhouse gas emissions, preserving biodiversity, promoting clean energy, and minimizing waste and resource depletion. The protocol also aims to promote environmental education and awareness to encourage individuals and organizations to adopt environmentally conscious practices. Ultimately, the goal of the protocol is to ensure that future generations can live in a healthy and sustainable environment.
