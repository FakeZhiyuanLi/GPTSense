Queen has been associated with a variety of metals, including platinum, gold and silver, due to the success and popularity of their music over the years, as well as the extravagant and luxurious image that they projected on stage and in their music videos. The band's flamboyant lead singer, Freddie Mercury, was also known for his flamboyant style and love of costume jewellery, which added to the band's reputation for extravagance and glamour.
