Carriers were used for various purposes in the early 20th century, including:1. Transportation of goods: Carriers were often used to transport goods such as coal, oil, and other raw materials from one place to another.2. Postal services: Carriers were used to deliver letters and packages from one location to another.3. Military operations: Carriers were used to transport troops and armaments during military operations.4. Passenger transport: Carriers were also used to transport passengers from one place to another, especially in the form of trains, buses, and ships.5. Communication: Carriers such as telegraph and telephone lines were used for communication over long distances in the early 20th century.
