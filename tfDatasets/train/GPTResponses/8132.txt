The majority of the eastern Sichuan province is made up of the Sichuan Basin, which is a low-lying area with fertile soil and a humid climate. It is known for its agricultural production, including rice, wheat, corn, and tea, as well as for its industry, which includes automobile manufacturing and electronics production. The area is also home to several cities, including Chengdu, the provincial capital, and Chongqing, which is a major industrial center and transportation hub.
