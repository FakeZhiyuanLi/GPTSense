Sauerkraut can be served as a side dish with sausages, hot dogs, corned beef and Reuben sandwiches. It can also be added to grilled cheese sandwiches, salads, soups and stews. In some cultures, sauerkraut is also served with meat dishes like pork chops, roasted chicken, and roasted pork loin. Additionally, sauerkraut can be used as a topping for vegan or vegetarian dishes or as a filling for pierogies and stuffed cabbage rolls.
