Yes, Freemasonry allows Catholics to join their fraternity. However, certain misunderstandings and disagreements between the Catholic Church and Freemasonry have led to the Church prohibiting its members from joining the organization. The situation varies depending on the local bishop and the country's laws regarding the relationship between the Church and Freemasonry. In some places, Catholics can join and participate in Freemasonry without any difficulty, while in others, it may not be allowed.
