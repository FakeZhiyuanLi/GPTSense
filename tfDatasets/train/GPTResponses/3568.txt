There are several countries in Europe that still mint gold coins, including:1. United Kingdom: The Royal Mint produces gold coins including the Britannia coin and the Sovereign coin.2. Austria: The Austrian Mint produces the Vienna Philharmonic gold coin.3. Switzerland: The Swiss Mint produces gold coins including the Gold Franc and the Gold Vreneli.4. France: The French Mint produces gold coins including the Napoleon III gold coin.5. Germany: The German Mint produces gold coins including the Goldmark and the Ducat.6. Italy: The Italian Mint produces gold coins including the Italian Lira and the Italian Euro gold coin.7. Greece: The Greek Mint produces gold coins including the Greek Drachma and the Greek Euro gold coin.
