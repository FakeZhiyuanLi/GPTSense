Beer is generally sold in various types of containers such as:1. Bottles - commonly found in 12-oz to 22-oz sizes2. Cans - traditionally 12-oz sizes but also available in larger formats, such as 16-oz and 24-oz3. Draft - served on tap, commonly sold in bars and restaurants4. Kegs - larger containers used for events, parties, or bars5. Growlers - refillable containers typically used for takeout from breweries or taprooms.
