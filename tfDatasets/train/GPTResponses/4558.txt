There are a few terms that can refer to modern texts written in Old English:1. Modern Old English: This term simply describes any modern writing that uses Old English vocabulary, grammar, and syntax.2. Neo-Old English: Similar to Modern Old English, this term emphasizes the neo (meaning "new") aspect of the writing.3. Anglish: This is a specific form of writing that seeks to use only English words of Germanic origin, including Old English. Anglish is often described as an "English purism" movement.4. Archaic English: While this term can refer to any type of archaic English writing, it can also be used to describe modern writing that mimics the style of Old English.5. Revived English: This term describes any attempt to revive an old form of English, including Old English.
