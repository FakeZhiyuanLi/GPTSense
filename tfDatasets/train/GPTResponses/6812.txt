The youngest daughter of Earl and Countess of Strathmore and Kinghorne was Lady Anne Mountbatten-Windsor, born on August 15, 1950.However, it should be noted that the Earl and Countess of Strathmore and Kinghorne are not currently active titles, as the most recent Earl passed away in 1944. The current title holder is Simon Bowes-Lyon, 19th Earl of Strathmore and Kinghorne.
