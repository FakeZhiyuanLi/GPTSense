Camp Lejeune is a United States Marine Corps base located in Jacksonville, North Carolina. It was established in 1941 and is named after Lieutenant General John A. Lejeune, the 13th Commandant of the Marine Corps. The base is home to several units and commands, including the II Marine Expeditionary Force, Marine Corps Base Camp Lejeune, and Marine Corps Installations East. Camp Lejeune provides training and support to Marines and their families, as well as conducts operations in support of national security.
