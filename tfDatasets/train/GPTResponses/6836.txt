Apart from a designated manager, other individuals who can perform some of the manager's roles and responsibilities include:1. Supervisors or team leaders: They may have limited authority, but they are responsible for managing a team or department.2. Senior employees: They may have the expertise, knowledge, and skills necessary to manage certain aspects of the business.3. Project managers: They are responsible for leading and managing specific projects within a company.4. Consultants: They can be hired to provide expertise and guidance on specific business processes or projects.5. Partners or co-founders: In a small business, partners or co-founders may share managerial responsibilities.6. Administration staff: They can help manage certain aspects of the business, such as accounting, human resources, and logistics.
