Houston is a major transportation hub with numerous types of roads converging within the city. These include:1. Interstates: Houston is served by two major interstate highways, I-45 and I-10.2. State Highways: Houston has several state highways that connect within the city, including State Highway 288, State Highway 249, and State Highway 6.3. Toll Roads: Houston has several toll roads that converge within the city, including the Sam Houston Tollway, the Westpark Tollway, and the Grand Parkway Toll Road.4. Major Arterial Roads: Houston also has several major arterial roads, including Westheimer Road, Memorial Drive, and Bissonnet Street.5. Local Streets: Finally, Houston's local streets network also converges within the city, providing connectivity to neighborhoods and commercial areas throughout the city.
