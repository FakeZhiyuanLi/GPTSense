Markers can help create various things depending on the context in which they are used, such as:1. Visual art and design: markers are often used by artists and designers to create drawings, illustrations, sketches, and coloring.2. Education: markers can be used by teachers to facilitate brainstorming, mind-mapping, and note-taking.3. Presentations: markers can be used during presentations or meetings to highlight important points on flipcharts or whiteboards.4. DIY projects: markers can be used in various DIY projects like labeling jars, creating cards or tags, decorating fabrics, making signs, and more.5. Writing: markers can be used to create artistic lettering, calligraphy, or to highlight notes or passages in books.In short, markers offer versatility and ease of use for a range of tasks where a visible or colorful medium is needed.
