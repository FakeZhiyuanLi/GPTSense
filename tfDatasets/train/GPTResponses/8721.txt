Many individuals and groups wanted the Russian forces to leave the Principalities, including:1. The Ottoman Empire - the Principalities were technically part of the Ottoman Empire and were occupied by the Russian Empire during the Crimean War.2. The Romanian Nationalists - they wanted to unite the Principalities and form a single independent state, but saw Russian presence as a hindrance.3. Western European Powers - they saw Russian occupation as a threat to the balance of power in Europe and wanted their withdrawal.4. Some of the local population - while some welcomed the Russian forces as liberators, others saw them as an occupying force and wanted them to leave.
