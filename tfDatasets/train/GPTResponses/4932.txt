Kullu is famous for its scenic beauty, adventure activities such as trekking, river rafting, paragliding, camping, and skiing. It is also known for its ancient temples and traditional festivals such as Kullu Dussehra and the International Folk Festival. The region is also renowned for its colorful handicrafts such as shawls, caps, and carpets made by local artisans. Kullu is popularly known as the 'Valley of Gods' as it is home to many ancient temples dedicated to various Hindu deities.
