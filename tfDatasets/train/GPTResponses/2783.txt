Some other sources used in Somali studies are:1) Oral tradition or oral history: This is the transmission of historical knowledge from one generation to another verbally. It includes folklore, proverbs, genealogies, and poetry.2) Islamic sources: These are religious texts such as the Quran and Hadith, which have influenced Somali culture and society.3) Archaeological sources: These are physical remains of past human activities such as ancient ruins, artifacts, and fossils.4) Linguistic sources: The Somali language and its various dialects provide insight into Somali culture, history, and societal practices.5) Ethnographic sources: These are studies of the culture and daily life of Somali communities, which provide valuable information on traditional practices and social organization.
