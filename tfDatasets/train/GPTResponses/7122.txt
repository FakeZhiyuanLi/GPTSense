Presbyterians exhibit their faith in several ways, including:1. Worship - attending church services regularly and participating in congregational singing, prayer, and reading of Scripture.2. Fellowship - building relationships with other Christians by participating in church activities, outreach programs, and community events.3. Bible Study - studying the Scriptures, attending Bible study groups, and participating in Christian education classes.4. Service - engaging in acts of service within their church and community, such as volunteering, taking part in outreach programs, and helping those in need.5. Stewardship - giving financially and generously to their church, ministries, and other charitable causes.6. Evangelism - sharing the gospel message with others and inviting them to faith in Christ.
