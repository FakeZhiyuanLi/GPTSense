Besides militarism, the state of Israel places emphasis on various aspects such as economic development, innovation and technology, education, healthcare, and international relations. Israel has a flourishing tech industry and is known for its inventions and innovations in fields such as cybersecurity, biotechnology, and renewable energy. Education and healthcare are also priorities, with Israel having some of the highest standards in both areas. The country also places importance on maintaining positive relationships with other countries through diplomacy and international aid efforts. Additionally, Israel emphasizes its cultural and historical heritage, promoting tourism and preserving important religious and cultural sites for visitors.
