Examples of a cell's external environment include:1. Surrounding fluids (such as blood or interstitial fluid)2. Nutrients in extracellular fluid3. Oxygen concentration in extracellular fluid4. pH of extracellular fluid5. Temperature6. External pressure7. Presence of other cells or microorganisms8. Presence of toxins or foreign molecules9. Light or electromagnetic radiation (for some cells, such as photoreceptor cells in the eye).
