The Bible mentions migration in several places, including:1. Genesis 12:1-4 - God instructs Abram (later known as Abraham) to leave his homeland and go to a new land that God will show him.2. Exodus 12:37-39 - The Israelites migrate from Egypt to the promised land of Canaan.3. Ruth 1:1-5 - Ruth migrates from Moab to Israel with her mother-in-law Naomi.4. Matthew 2:13-15 - Joseph, Mary, and Jesus migrate to Egypt to escape King Herod's tyranny.5. Acts 8:1-4 - The disciples of Jesus migrate from Jerusalem to other parts of the world, spreading the gospel message.
