Small missiles can be designed and mounted on a variety of platforms such as:1. Aircraft: Small missiles can be mounted on fighter jets, helicopters, unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) for air-to-air or air-to-ground attacks.2. Ground vehicles: Missiles can be mounted on tanks, armored personnel carriers (APCs), or other military vehicles for ground-based attacks.3. Ships: Missiles can be mounted on naval ships, submarines, or surface-to-air missile systems for naval warfare.4. Portable launchers: Small missiles can be designed to be portable and mounted on ground-based launchers, making them more mobile and easily deployable in varying environments.Overall, small missiles provide a flexible and adaptable weapon system that can be used in a wide range of scenarios and situations.
