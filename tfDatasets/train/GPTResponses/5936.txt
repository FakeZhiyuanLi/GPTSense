Smaller organizations are responsible for managing their own finances, operations, and workforce. This may include:1. Financial management: Smaller organizations are responsible for managing their own finances. This includes budgeting, accounting, bookkeeping, tax filing, and financial reporting.2. Operation management: Smaller organizations are responsible for managing their operations efficiently. This may include procurement, logistics, inventory management, and supply chain management.3. Workforce management: Smaller organizations are responsible for managing their own workforce. This includes hiring, training, and employee management.4. Compliance: Smaller organizations are responsible for complying with laws and regulations related to their business activities. This may include environmental laws, labor laws, and data privacy laws.5. Customer service: Smaller organizations are responsible for providing excellent customer service to their clients, which includes meeting their needs and resolving complaints in a timely and efficient manner.
