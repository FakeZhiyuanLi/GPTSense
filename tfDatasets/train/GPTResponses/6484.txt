Some dimensions of sexual orientation include:1. Sexual attraction: The gender(s) to which a person is attracted2. Sexual behavior: The gender(s) with whom a person has sexual interactions3. Sexual identity: The label or category that a person uses to describe their sexual orientation, such as heterosexual, homosexual, bisexual, or pansexual4. Romantic attraction: The gender(s) to which a person is emotionally and romantically attracted5. Gender identity: The gender with which a person identifies, which may or may not be aligned with their assigned sex at birth.
