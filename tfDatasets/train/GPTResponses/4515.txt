Copper compounds in liquid form are used for a variety of purposes, including:1. As fungicides and bactericides in agriculture and horticulture to control plant diseases.2. In wood preservatives to protect against decay and insect attacks.3. In water treatment to control algae growth and other microorganisms.4. In electroplating and surface treatments to enhance the properties of metals.5. In pigments and dyes for coloring ceramics, glass, and other materials.6. In lubricants to reduce friction and wear.7. In chemical synthesis and catalysis as catalysts or reagents.8. In the production of semiconductors and electronic components.9. In the manufacture of batteries and other electrical devices.10. In the production of copper-based alloys, such as brass and bronze.
