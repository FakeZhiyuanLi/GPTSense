Aircraft carriers allow naval forces to accomplish a variety of tasks, including:1. Power Projection: Carriers are capable of projecting power across vast distances, allowing naval forces to quickly respond to crises and conflicts.2. Air Superiority: Carriers provide air superiority to naval forces, enabling them to control the skies and protect other ships in the fleet.3. Surveillance and Intelligence Gathering: Carriers are equipped with advanced surveillance and intelligence gathering equipment, allowing naval forces to monitor the activities of potential threats.4. Rapid Strike Capability: Carriers can quickly launch strike missions against enemy targets, using a variety of weapons and systems.5. Humanitarian Assistance and Disaster Relief: Carriers can also serve as platforms for providing humanitarian assistance and disaster relief, using their aircraft to deliver aid and supplies to affected areas.
