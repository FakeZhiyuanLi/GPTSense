There were a number of individuals and institutions that supported people adding to the written knowledge of nature. These included:1. Patrons: Wealthy individuals and organizations often supported naturalists and scientists, providing them with financial resources and access to equipment and materials.2. Universities and Research Institutions: Many universities and research institutions employed naturalists and scientists, providing them with resources and facilities to support their research.3. Publishers: Publishers played an important role in bringing the written knowledge of nature to a wider audience. They often provided financial support to naturalists and scientists to publish their work.4. Scientific Societies: Scientific societies provided a space for naturalists and scientists to share and discuss their work, as well as funding opportunities for research.5. Museums and Botanical Gardens: Museums and botanical gardens often employed naturalists and scientists to conduct research and curate collections. They also provided educational opportunities for the public to learn about the natural world.
