There have been many struggles for independence or overthrowing prevailing governments throughout history, and it is difficult to pinpoint a specific time period. Some notable examples include:- The American Revolution (1775-1783), in which the 13 British colonies in North America fought for independence and formed the United States of America.- The French Revolution (1789-1799), in which the French people overthrew the absolute monarchy and established a republic.- The Latin American Wars of Independence (1810-1825), in which various Latin American countries fought for independence from Spain and Portugal.- The Russian Revolution (1917), in which the Bolsheviks overthrew the Russian Provisional Government and established the Soviet Union.- The decolonization movements of the 20th century, in which many countries in Africa, Asia, and the Caribbean gained independence from their colonial powers.These are just a few examples, and there have been countless other struggles for independence or overthrowing prevailing governments throughout history.
