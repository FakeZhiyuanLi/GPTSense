Various things can create sparks and explosions, such as:1. Fireworks - they are designed to release sparks and colors in the sky.2. Firearms - when a gun is fired, sparks and muzzle flashes are created.3. Electrical circuits - faulty wires or overloaded circuits can cause sparks and explosions.4. Chemical reactions - certain chemicals can produce sparks and explosions when they react with each other.5. Lightning - when lightning strikes, it creates a bright flash and explosion of thunder.6. Combustible materials - fuels such as gasoline or propane can ignite and create explosions.7. Pyrotechnics - theatrical effects such as special effects in movies or stage productions often use explosives and pyrotechnics to create explosions and sparks.
