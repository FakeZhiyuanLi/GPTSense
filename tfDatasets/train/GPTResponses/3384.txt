Some publications that help a dialect to become standardized include:1. Style guides: These provide guidelines for writing and formatting texts, including spelling, punctuation, and grammar rules.2. Academic papers and research articles: These help to promote a standard dialect by establishing conventions for vocabulary and usage.3. Media: Newspapers, magazines, and other forms of media can help to standardize a dialect through their use of language.4. Literary works: Works of fiction and poetry can help establish standard forms of language use, as well as contribute to the development of a literary canon for a particular dialect.5. Government documents: Official government documents and publications can help to promote standardization of language use within a particular region or country.
