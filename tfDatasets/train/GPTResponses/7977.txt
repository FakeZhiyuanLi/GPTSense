Shen Kuo, a polymath of the Song dynasty, made observations and documented various natural phenomena, including climate change. He studied records of historical weather patterns, observed the changes in the climate and the changes in the flora and fauna of various regions. Additionally, he analyzed the growth patterns of trees and the composition of rocks to understand changes in the environment. Shen Kuo also observed the varying amounts of rainfall and the movement of the celestial bodies, all of which contributed to his understanding of climate change.
