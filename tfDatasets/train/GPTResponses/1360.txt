There are many different types of music that come from oral tradition, as it varies depending on the culture and location. Some examples include:- African drumming and chant- Celtic folk music- Indian classical music- Japanese Taiko drumming- Native American powwow drumming and singing- Tibetan throat singing- Appalachian folk music- Andean panpipe music- Indonesian gamelan music- Turkish Sufi musicThese are just a few examples of the diverse range of music that comes from oral tradition.
