There are several nationalities that have increased their city populations due to new industries. Some examples include:1. Indians: With the growth of the IT industry in cities like Bangalore, Hyderabad, and Chennai, there has been a significant increase in the number of Indians moving to these cities for work.2. Filipinos: The BPO industry in the Philippines has led to an influx of Filipinos moving to cities like Manila, Cebu, and Davao for work.3. Chinese: China's rapid economic growth has led to many Chinese moving to cities like Beijing, Shanghai, and Guangzhou for work opportunities.4. Nigerians: With the growth of the oil industry in cities like Lagos and Port Harcourt, there has been an increase in the number of Nigerians moving to these cities for work.5. Mexicans: The automotive industry in cities like Guadalajara and Monterrey has led to an influx of Mexicans moving to these cities for work.
