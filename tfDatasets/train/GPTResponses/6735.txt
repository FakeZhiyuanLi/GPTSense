There are several cases in which a referee's decision can be overturned:1. Video review: In some sports, video review can be used to check the validity of a decision made on the field. If the video evidence shows that the decision was incorrect, it may be overturned.2. Rule violation: If the referee makes a decision based on a faulty understanding of the rules or if they violate a rule themselves, the decision may be overturned.3. Appeal: In some cases, a decision may be appealed to a higher authority, such as a league or governing body. If the appeal shows that the decision was incorrect, it may be overturned.4. Improper conduct: If a referee engages in improper conduct, such as bribery or favoritism, their decisions may be overturned.
