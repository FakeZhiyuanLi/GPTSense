According to the US News & World Report's 2021-2022 rankings, MD Anderson Cancer Center is the top-ranked hospital in the United States for cancer care. It has held this position for 17 consecutive years. The hospital has a team of experts and offers many innovative treatments for various types of cancer, including surgery, radiation therapy, chemotherapy, and immunotherapy. The hospital has consistently been recognized for its exceptional patient care, advanced research, and cutting-edge technologies.
