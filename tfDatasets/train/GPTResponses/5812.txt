Each grand lodge maintains the following:1. Membership records: They keep records of their members, including their names, addresses, and other relevant information.2. Rituals and ceremonies: They develop and maintain the rituals and ceremonies that are used in the lodges.3. Masonic education: They are responsible for providing educational resources and programs to their members.4. Charitable work: They oversee the charitable work of the lodges and ensure that the funds raised are used for worthy causes.5. Governance: They provide leadership and set policies that govern the lodges under their jurisdiction.6. Masonic buildings: They own and maintain the buildings where the lodges meet.7. Recognition: They are responsible for recognizing other grand lodges and local lodges within their jurisdiction.
