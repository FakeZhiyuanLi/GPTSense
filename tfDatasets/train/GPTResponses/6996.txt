During an interview, the interviewer will typically ask the candidate questions to learn more about their skills, qualifications, and experience. The candidate may also be asked about their personal and professional goals, their strengths and weaknesses, and their work style. The interviewer may also explain the company's values, culture, and expectations. Depending on the position, the candidate may be asked to provide examples of their work or to complete a skills test. The interview may also include discussing salary, benefits, and upcoming steps in the hiring process. Additionally, the candidate may ask questions about the company and the role to better understand if it is a good fit for them.
