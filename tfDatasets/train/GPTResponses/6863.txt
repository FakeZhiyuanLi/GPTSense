Apizza is a type of pizza that originated in New Haven, Connecticut. It is known for its thin, chewy, and crispy crust, which is often cooked in a wood-fired oven at a high temperature to give it a unique flavor and texture. Apizza is also typically topped with a simple tomato sauce, grated cheese, and other toppings such as pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, and peppers. It is considered a regional specialty and has gained a loyal following among pizza connoisseurs.
