The evidence of ancient cultures was discovered in various forms, including:1. Artifacts: These are objects made or used by people in ancient times, such as tools, weapons, pottery, clothing, and jewelry.2. Architecture: Buildings, monuments, and other structures are examples of architectural evidence that provide insight into the engineering and construction techniques of ancient civilizations.3. Written records: Ancient texts, manuscripts, and inscriptions are valuable sources of information about the customs, beliefs, and achievements of ancient societies.4. Fossils: The scientific study of fossils helps us understand the evolution of life on Earth over millions of years.5. Oral traditions: Folklore, mythologies, and other oral traditions also provide important clues about the lives and cultures of ancient people.
