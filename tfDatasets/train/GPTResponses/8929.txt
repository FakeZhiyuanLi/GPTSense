One good source of marine research in Cork is the MaREI Centre for Energy, Climate and Marine. It is a research center located in University College Cork and specializes in research related to marine renewable energy, climate change, and coastal and marine ecosystems. They collaborate with a network of universities, research institutions, and industry partners, including international partners. MaREI is also home to the Beaufort Building, a state-of-the-art research facility that houses world-class marine research equipment and laboratories.
