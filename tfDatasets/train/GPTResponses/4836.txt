The USS America is a versatile amphibious assault ship that is capable of providing air defense and support for ground troops. It is equipped with a range of weapons, including missiles, guns, and aircraft, that can engage and destroy enemy squadrons of planes and helicopters. The USS America can also launch and recover drones and unmanned aerial vehicles, which can provide additional intelligence, surveillance, and reconnaissance capabilities. Overall, the USS America is a powerful and flexible asset that can operate in a variety of mission scenarios and environments.
