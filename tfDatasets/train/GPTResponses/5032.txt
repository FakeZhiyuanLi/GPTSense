There are several international political issues that are currently becoming increasingly prominent:1. Climate change - The global community is grappling with the urgent need to address the impact of climate change and reduce emissions.2. COVID-19 pandemic- The pandemic has highlighted the need for international cooperation and coordination in terms of public health, trade, and security.3. Immigration - The issue of migration and refugees has become a global concern as people are forced to flee their homes due to conflict, persecution, and economic challenges.4. Cybersecurity - With the increasing number of cyber attacks, the issue of cybersecurity has become an international concern, with governments and organizations seeking to establish norms and standards for cybersecurity.5. Trade disputes - The growing number of trade disputes between countries, especially between the US and China, is a global concern that may lead to political tensions and economic disruption.
