Unicode allows characters to be represented as a single character or as a decomposed sequence of two or more combining characters. Some characters that can be displayed both as a single character or a decomposed sequence are:1. À - Latin capital letter A with grave accent2. á - Latin small letter a with acute accent3. ç - Latin small letter c with cedilla4. ß - German small letter sharp s (eszett)5. ә - Cyrillic small letter schwa6. ღ - Georgian letter ghan7. ళ - Telugu letter lla8. 꿀 - Hangul letter ssanghieuh (double hieuh)
