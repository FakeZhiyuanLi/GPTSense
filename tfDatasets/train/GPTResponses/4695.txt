Shooters are responsible for the proper handling and use of firearms, including ensuring they are unloaded and safe when not in use, using appropriate safety precautions while handling firearms, and following all local, state, and federal firearms laws and regulations. They are also responsible for the safe storage and transport of firearms, as well as for ensuring that only authorized individuals are able to access and use them. Overall, shooters have a responsibility to prioritize safety and prevent any harm or damage caused by firearms.
