1. Insects: Pesticides are used to control a variety of insects, including bedbugs, mosquitoes, termites, and cockroaches. These insects can cause damage to crops, spread diseases, or become a nuisance in homes and businesses.2. Weeds: Pesticides target weeds that compete with crops for resources like nutrients, sunlight, and water. Herbicides are used to kill unwanted weeds in agricultural lands, lawns, gardens, and other areas.3. Rodents: Pesticides can help control rodents such as rats and mice, which can spread diseases, contaminate food, and cause damage to property. Rodenticides are used to eliminate these pests in homes, businesses, and farms.
