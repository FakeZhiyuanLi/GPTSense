Wrestling serves various purposes and fills different roles, including:1. Physical fitness and development: Wrestling is an intensive sport that requires strength, endurance, and flexibility, which can help individuals develop physical fitness.2. Mental and emotional development: Wrestling can teach discipline, sportsmanship, where to draw the line - providing a sense of stress relief and emotional release.3. Social engagement: Wrestling can bring people together who share a common interest and serve as a way of making new friends and building relationships. Being part of a club or team can encourage communication and teamwork.4. Entertainment: Wrestling can provide entertainment, particularly for fans of the sport who enjoy watching professional wrestling matches, making it a popular form of entertainment.5. Self-defense: Wrestling techniques and skills can be used as self-defense mechanisms in real-life situations.Overall, wrestling serves as a platform for individuals to build self-confidence, develop their skills, and explore their physical and mental capabilities.
