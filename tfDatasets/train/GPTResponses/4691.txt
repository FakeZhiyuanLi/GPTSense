1. Pilot-in-Command (PIC) - The PIC is responsible for the operation and safety of the aircraft, and has ultimate authority over all decisions made in the flight.2. First Officer (FO) - The FO assists the PIC in all aspects of the flight, and is responsible for operating the aircraft's systems and instruments.3. Flight Engineer (FE) - Some older aircraft, particularly cargo and military planes, have a flight engineer who monitors, operates and troubleshoots the mechanical and electrical systems of the aircraft. However, this position has become largely obsolete due to advanced technology in modern aircraft.
