Unicode contains a vast array of combinations in normal use, including:- Letters from various alphabets, such as Latin, Cyrillic, Greek, Arabic, Hebrew, Chinese, Japanese, and Korean- Various punctuation marks, such as periods, commas, exclamation marks, question marks, parentheses, brackets, dashes, and quotation marks- Numerals from various numeral systems, such as Arabic numerals, Roman numerals, and various other number symbols- Various symbols and icons, such as currency symbols, arrows, emojis, and mathematical symbols- Various diacritics and accents, such as accents, cedillas, umlauts, and tildes, used to modify letters- Various formatting characters, such as line breaks, tabs, and spaces, used to structure text- Various control characters, such as the newline character and the backspace character, used to control the display and processing of text.
