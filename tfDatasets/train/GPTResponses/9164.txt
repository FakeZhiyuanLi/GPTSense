Some examples of conquests that were gradual in nature are:1. Roman conquests: Rome's expansion throughout Europe, Africa, and Asia was a gradual process that took centuries to complete. Rome started as a small city-state and gradually built an empire.2. Spanish conquest of the Americas: The Spanish gradually conquered the Americas beginning in the late 15th century. It took them several decades to conquer major empires like the Aztecs and Incas.3. Viking conquests: The Viking expansion from Scandinavia throughout Europe happened gradually over several centuries between the 8th and 11th century.4. Islamic conquests: The spread of Islam throughout the Middle East, North Africa, and Europe was a gradual process that took several centuries, beginning in the 7th century.5. British imperial conquests: The British Empire expanded its territory gradually over several centuries through colonization and conquest, which included the Americas, Africa, Asia, and Oceania.
