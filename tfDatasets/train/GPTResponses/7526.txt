Bermuda's largest economic sectors are:1. International Business and Finance: Bermuda is a major offshore financial center, and its international business sector contributes significantly to the country's economy.2. Tourism: Bermuda's tourism industry is also a significant contributor to the economy, attracting visitors from around the world with its beautiful beaches, golf courses, and historic sites.3. Construction: With a growing population and a need for infrastructure upgrades and improvements, the construction industry is also an important player in Bermuda's economy.4. Retail and Wholesale Trade: The retail and wholesale trade sector includes a range of businesses, from small independent shops to large chain stores, and is an integral part of the country's economy.5. Government Services: As a British Overseas Territory, Bermuda relies heavily on public sector employment and services, with government jobs in areas like education and healthcare supporting the economy.
