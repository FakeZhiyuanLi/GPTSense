Some factors that can reduce the risk of new onset kidney disease and death include:1. Maintaining healthy blood pressure levels2. Managing blood sugar levels for diabetics3. Eating a healthy and balanced diet4. Regular exercise and physical activity5. Avoiding smoking and excessive alcohol consumption6. Regular health checkups and screening for kidney disease7. Avoiding over-the-counter pain medications like ibuprofen and naproxen8. Early treatment and management of kidney disease or existing health conditions that can lead to kidney disease9. Maintaining a healthy weight10. Staying hydrated by drinking plenty of water.
