There are several organizations that rank graduates according to their starting salary in the UK. Some of the most reliable sources include:1. The Higher Education Statistics Agency (HESA): HESA is the official government agency that collects and analyses data about higher education in the UK. They publish an annual report that includes information about graduate employment and salaries.2. The Complete University Guide: This organization publishes annual rankings of UK universities based on various factors, including graduate starting salaries.3. The Times Higher Education: This organization also ranks UK universities based on various factors, including graduate employment outcomes and salaries.4. The Guardian: This newspaper publishes a university league table that takes into account graduate employment and salaries.5. QS World University Rankings: This global ranking system also includes data on graduate employability and salaries for universities in the UK.
