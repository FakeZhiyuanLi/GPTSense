1. Copper: Portugal is the largest producer of copper in the European Union and the world's 10th largest producer. It is mined in the Alentejo region in the south of the country.2. Tungsten: Portugal is the world's largest producer of tungsten, a rare and valuable metal used in electrical wiring and machinery manufacturing. It is mainly mined in the northern region of Tras-os-Montes.3. Lithium: Portugal has significant deposits of lithium, a key ingredient in batteries for electric vehicles and energy storage. It is extracted from the Alto Minho region in the northwest of the country.
