The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Greece conducts a wide range of activities related to Greek foreign policy. These include:1. Protecting and promoting Greek interests and values abroad.2. Formulating and implementing Greek foreign policy.3. Maintaining diplomatic relations with other countries and international organizations.4. Representing Greece in international forums and negotiations.5. Providing consular assistance and protection to Greek citizens abroad.6. Promoting Greek culture and heritage internationally.7. Providing education and training in foreign policy and diplomacy.8. Conducting research and analysis on international relations and global issues.9. Managing Greece's participation in multilateral organizations such as the European Union, United Nations, and NATO.10. Cooperating with other countries and organizations to address various global challenges such as climate change, migration, and terrorism.
