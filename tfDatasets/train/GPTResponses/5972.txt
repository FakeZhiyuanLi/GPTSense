There are several online resources where Sanskrit broadcasts can be found:1. All India Radio: The official website of All India Radio (AIR) broadcasts Sanskrit programs on a daily basis. The website also has a section for podcasts where previous shows can be accessed.2. The Sanskrit Channel: This website streams 24/7 Sanskrit content, including music, lectures, and interviews.3. Samskrita Bharati: This website hosts a variety of Sanskrit resources, including podcasts, videos, articles, and books.4. Sanskrit Central: This website offers a vast collection of Sanskrit literature, including audiobooks, textbooks, and speeches.5. YouTube: There are many Sanskrit channels on YouTube that broadcast content in Sanskrit, including lectures, recitations, and music.
