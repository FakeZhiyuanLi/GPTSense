An MCU (Microcontroller Unit) can be embedded in various devices and systems some of them include:- Home appliances such as washing machines, refrigerators, ovens, and air conditioners- Industrial machines and equipment- Automotive systems such as engine control units, anti-lock brake systems, and dashboard displays- Medical devices such as insulin pumps, pacemakers, and blood glucose monitors- Smart home devices such as thermostats, security systems, and lighting control systems- Wearable devices such as fitness trackers and smartwatches- Consumer electronics such as digital cameras, drones, and gaming consoles- IoT devices such as sensors, gateways, and routers.
