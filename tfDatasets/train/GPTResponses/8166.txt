Bermuda has a strong tradition of participating in the Summer Olympics. The country made its debut in the Olympic Games in 1936 and since then has participated in every Summer Olympics except for the 1980 Moscow Games. Bermuda has not yet won a medal in the Olympics, but the country has produced several world-class athletes in different sports such as sailing, triathlon, and athletics. The Olympic tradition for Bermuda is to send a small but competitive team of athletes to the Summer Olympics to represent the country on the international stage.
