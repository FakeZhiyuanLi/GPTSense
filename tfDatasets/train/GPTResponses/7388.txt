There are several natural substances that have been found to effectively increase crop yields, including:1. Seaweed: Seaweed contains plant hormones and trace elements that can stimulate growth, improve root development, and increase crop yield.2. Humic acid: Humic acid is a natural substance found in soil that can enhance nutrient uptake in plants, improve soil structure, and increase water retention.3. Compost: Compost is a rich source of organic matter that can improve soil fertility, increase beneficial microorganisms, and enhance plant growth.4. Mycorrhizal fungi: Mycorrhizal fungi form a symbiotic relationship with plant roots, helping them to access nutrients and water more effectively and improving their resistance to stress.5. Neem oil: Neem oil is a natural pesticide and fungicide that can protect crops from pests and diseases, while also boosting their growth and yield.6. Fish emulsion: Fish emulsion is a fertilizer made from decomposed fish, and is a rich source of nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium, which are essential nutrients for plant growth.7. Molasses: Molasses is a natural source of sugar that can stimulate microbial activity in the soil, improving nutrient availability and plant growth.
