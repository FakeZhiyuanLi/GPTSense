When a wrestler enters the ring, several things occur:1. Their entrance music plays. This is the music that the wrestler has chosen to represent them and usually has a significant meaning behind it.2. The wrestler makes their way down to the ring. They may walk, run, or have a unique entrance, depending on their character.3. They climb into the ring. Wrestlers will usually climb up onto the apron and then over the top rope to get into the ring.4. They usually perform a signature pose or gesture. This is a way for the wrestler to get the crowd excited and to show off their personality.5. They await their opponent. Once both wrestlers are in the ring, the match can begin.
