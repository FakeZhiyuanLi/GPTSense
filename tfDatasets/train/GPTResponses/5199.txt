1. Time delay fuses: These fuses are designed to detonate after a specific time delay, which can range from a fraction of a second to several minutes. They are commonly used in anti-personnel and anti-tank mines as well as in airburst bombs.2. Proximity fuses: These fuses are designed to detect the proximity of a target, such as an aircraft or a missile, and detonate when the target is within a certain distance. They are commonly used in air-to-air and surface-to-air missiles as well as in artillery shells and bombs.
