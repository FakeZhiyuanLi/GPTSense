POTS can stand for several things depending on the context:1. Postural Orthostatic Tachycardia Syndrome - a medical condition characterized by symptoms such as lightheadedness, fainting, and rapid heart rate upon standing up.2. Plain Old Telephone Service - a term used to differentiate traditional analog telephone lines from newer digital phone services.3. Potentiometer - a type of variable resistor used to control the flow of electricity.4. Planetary Orbital Tracking System - a system used to track man-made objects in space.5. Power over Ethernet - a technology that allows electrical power and data to be transmitted over Ethernet cables.
