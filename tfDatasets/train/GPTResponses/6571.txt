Florida has various architectural styles including:1. Mediterranean Revival - characterized by red tile roofs, stucco walls, and decorative ironwork.2. Art Deco - characterized by geometric shapes, bold colors, and intricate details.3. Postmodernism - characterized by playful design elements, use of bright colors, and mixed materials.4. Ranch-style - characterized by a low profile, wide eaves, and open floor plans.5. Victorian - characterized by ornate details, steep gabled roofs, and balconies.6. Key West - characterized by colorful homes with steep-pitched roofs, decorative shutters, and wraparound porches.7. Contemporary - characterized by clean lines, sleek designs, and modern materials.8. Colonial Revival - characterized by symmetrical designs, columned porches, and brick or clapboard facades.
