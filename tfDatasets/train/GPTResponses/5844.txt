The annual Montevideo Carnaval is part of the cultural heritage of Uruguay and is a traditional celebration that takes place in the month of February. It is a colorful and vibrant festival that features music, dancing, and parades with elaborate costumes and masks. The Montevideo Carnaval is one of the most significant events in the country and attracts tourists and locals alike. It is a time for people to come together, have fun, and celebrate the rich culture of Uruguay.
