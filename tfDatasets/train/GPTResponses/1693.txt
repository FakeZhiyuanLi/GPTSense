Barbarian invaders established kingdoms in various states throughout history. Some examples include:- The Visigoths established the Visigothic Kingdom in Spain in the 5th century AD.- The Ostrogoths established the Ostrogothic Kingdom in Italy in the 5th century AD.- The Franks established the Frankish Kingdom in what is now France and Germany in the 6th century AD.- The Angles and Saxons established Anglo-Saxon kingdoms in what is now England in the 5th and 6th centuries AD.- The Lombards established the Lombard Kingdom in Italy in the 6th century AD.These are just a few examples and there are many others throughout history.
