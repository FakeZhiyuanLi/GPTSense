The Carnival of Gualeguaychú, in the province of Entre Ríos, is the most representative carnival in Argentina in terms of the nature of the carnival. The carnival takes place during the weekends of January, February, and early March, where groups of dancers and musicians perform on stage with elaborate costumes, music, and dancing. The Carnival of Gualeguaychú celebrates creativity, tradition, and community spirit, with its focus on artistic expression and cultural identity.
