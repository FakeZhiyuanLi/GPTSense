As Victoria's rule became less political, the values that were emphasized included social mores, family values, morality, and personal responsibility. Victorian society placed a strong emphasis on upholding traditional values, respecting authority, and maintaining social order. There was an emphasis on the importance of education, hard work, and self-reliance. The values of modesty, decorum, and propriety were also highly valued in Victorian society, particularly among women. Additionally, philanthropy and charitable giving were considered important values.
