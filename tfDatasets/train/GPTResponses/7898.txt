The medicine practiced in India before 2500 BC is known as Ayurveda. Ayurveda is an ancient system of medicine that emphasizes on the holistic approach to maintaining health and preventing diseases. It is based on the belief that good health comes from a balance between the mind, body, and spirit. Ayurvedic medicine involves the use of herbs, minerals, and other natural remedies to treat various ailments. It also includes practices such as yoga, meditation, and massage to promote overall well-being. Ayurveda has been practiced in India for over 5,000 years and is still widely used today.
