Yale-New Haven Hospital is a renowned hospital in New Haven that provides notable cardiac emergency care with a team of expert cardiologists, surgeons, and other specialists who are dedicated to providing the highest level of care for cardiac emergencies such as heart attacks, arrhythmias, and other heart-related conditions. The hospital also has advanced technology and facilities for cardiac care, including a cardiac catheterization lab, electrophysiology lab, and state-of-the-art imaging equipment.
