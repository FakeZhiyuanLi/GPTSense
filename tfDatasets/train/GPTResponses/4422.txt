Some negative experiences that peer pressure can influence during adolescence are:1. Substance abuse: Adolescents may feel the need to try drugs or alcohol to fit in with their peers, which can lead to addiction and other health problems.2. Risky behavior: Peer pressure can lead adolescents to engage in risky behavior such as skipping school, stealing, or having unprotected sex.3. Bullying: Adolescents may feel pressure to bully others or become a victim of bullying from peers.4. Academic performance: Peer pressure to prioritize social activities over academic ones can negatively impact an adolescent's academic performance.5. Negative self-image: Adolescents may feel pressured to conform to certain beauty standards or social norms, leading to negative self-image and self-esteem issues.
