Other migration hazards include:1. Physical dangers during the migration journey such as extreme weather conditions, violence, and accidents.2. Exploitation and human trafficking by smugglers or other criminal groups.3. Lack of access to basic necessities such as food, water, shelter, and medical care.4. Discrimination and xenophobia in the destination country, which may lead to social exclusion and challenges in accessing education, employment, and healthcare.5. Separation of families and disruptions to social networks and support systems.6. Psychological trauma resulting from the stress of migration and the experience of leaving behind one's home, culture, and identity.
