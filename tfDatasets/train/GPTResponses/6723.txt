Small scale infringement disputes can be resolved through various methods including negotiation, mediation, arbitration, and legal action.- Negotiation: This involves both parties discussing the issues and finding an agreement that satisfies both parties without involving a third party.- Mediation: This involves a neutral third party (a mediator) who helps the parties to have a constructive conversation and reach a mutually acceptable resolution.- Arbitration: This involves a neutral third party (an arbitrator) who hears both sides of the dispute and makes a decision that is binding on both parties.- Legal Action: This involves going to court and having a judge issue a ruling on the matter. However, legal action can be costly and time-consuming.The most appropriate method for resolving the dispute will depend on the specific circumstances of the case.
