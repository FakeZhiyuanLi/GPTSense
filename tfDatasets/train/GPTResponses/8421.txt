There are still several debates surrounding issues related to African Americans, including:1. The effectiveness of affirmative action policies in addressing the under representation of African Americans and other minority groups in employment and education.2. The extent to which systemic racism and implicit biases contribute to the persistent disparities in income, education, and health outcomes among African Americans.3. How best to address the high rate of incarceration and criminalization of African Americans, particularly in the context of the ongoing debate around police reform and the Black Lives Matter movement.4. The role of cultural, social, and economic factors in shaping African American identity and community, including debates around the importance of historically black colleges and universities, black churches, and other institutions.5. The ongoing legacy of slavery and racial discrimination in American society, including debates around reparations and other forms of redress for historical injustices.
