Bermuda is considered an offshore financial center because it is known for its favorable tax laws and regulations that attract foreign investors, corporations, and wealthy individuals. The country offers low taxes, strong confidentiality laws, and a stable political and economic environment, making it an attractive location for setting up offshore companies, trusts, and other offshore financial structures. Additionally, Bermuda has a sophisticated financial infrastructure, with a well-regulated banking system, and world-class professional services firms, making it an ideal location for managing and investing financial assets.
