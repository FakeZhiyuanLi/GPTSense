The earliest texts describing meditation techniques are found in the Hindu scriptures, specifically the Vedas and the Upanishads. These texts date back to around 2000 BCE and describe various forms of meditation, such as breath control and mantra repetition.The Buddhist scriptures, such as the Pali Canon, also describe meditation techniques that were practiced around the fifth century BCE. These techniques include mindfulness meditation and concentration meditation.The Taoist text, the Tao Te Ching, also describes meditation techniques that were practiced around the fourth century BCE, such as sitting in stillness and emptying the mind.Overall, these ancient texts provide a rich source of information on meditation practices that have been used for thousands of years.
