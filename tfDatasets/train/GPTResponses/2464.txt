Water can be diverted from rivers for various purposes, including:1. Irrigation: Water is diverted from rivers to irrigate crops and maintain agricultural lands.2. Domestic use: Water is diverted for households, drinking, washing, and other purposes.3. Power generation: Water is diverted to create hydroelectric power, which is an important source of renewable energy.4. Industrial use: Water is diverted for industrial processes, such as manufacturing, mining, and chemical processing.5. Navigation: Water is diverted to maintain navigable waterways and support shipping and transportation.6. Recreation: Water is diverted to support recreational activities such as swimming, boating, and fishing.7. Environmental protection: Water is diverted to protect habitat, wetlands, and other sensitive ecosystems.
