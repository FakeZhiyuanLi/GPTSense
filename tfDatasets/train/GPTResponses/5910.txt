Urban Melbourne is divided into hundreds of suburbs for several reasons:1) Historical reasons: The suburbs of Melbourne evolved from small towns and villages that were annexed by the city as it grew.2) Administrative purposes: Suburbs are used for administrative purposes such as the collection of rates, planning decisions and service delivery.3) Housing developments: New housing developments are often established as suburbs, with each suburb having its own unique identity.4) Community identity: Suburbs provide a sense of community identity and pride, with residents identifying with their local area and working together to improve it.5) Geographic delineation: Suburbs provide a geographical delineation of the city, making it easier for people to understand the layout of Melbourne and navigate around the city.Overall, the division of Melbourne into hundreds of suburbs has helped to create a diverse and dynamic city with a strong sense of community identity.
