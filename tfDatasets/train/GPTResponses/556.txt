There are many different types of dogs that help people with physical or mental disabilities. Some of the most popular include:1. Guide dogs: These dogs are trained to help people who are blind or visually impaired navigate the world around them.2. Hearing dogs: These dogs are trained to assist people who are deaf or hard of hearing by alerting them to important sounds like doorbells, alarms, and approaching people or cars.3. Service dogs: These dogs are trained to perform a variety of tasks to help people with disabilities, such as pulling wheelchairs, opening doors, and picking up dropped items.4. Autism support dogs: These dogs are trained to help people with autism by providing stability, comfort, and companionship.5. Psychiatric service dogs: These dogs are trained to help people with mental health conditions like anxiety, depression, or PTSD by providing emotional support, calming behaviors, and assistance during panic attacks.
