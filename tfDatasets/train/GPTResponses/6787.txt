The dress for a king's funeral varies depending on the country and culture. However, in many cases, formal attire is worn, such as black suits, ties, and formal black dresses. In some cultures, people may wear traditional funeral attire, such as white clothing in Hindu funerals or embroidered garments in Chinese funerals. In some countries, military dress may also be worn by members of the armed forces. Ultimately, the attire worn at a king's funeral is meant to pay respects, show mourning, and honor the late king.
