A tribunal or an arbiter might be asked to determine the following regarding a breach of a treaty:1. Whether a breach has occurred.2. The extent or severity of the breach.3. The party or parties responsible for the breach.4. The damages or compensation the injured party is entitled to receive as a result of the breach.5. The appropriate remedies or sanctions to be imposed on the breaching party or parties.6. Whether the breached treaty is still in effect or has become void.7. Whether the breach of the treaty constitutes a violation of international law.8. Whether the parties can come to an agreement through non-judicial means, such as mediation or negotiation, to resolve the breach.
