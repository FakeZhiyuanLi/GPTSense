Garze County and Zoige County in Sichuan experience a sub-frigid-continental plateau climate, with long and cold winters and short and cool summers. The average annual temperature ranges from -5 to 9°C (23 to 48°F), with January being the coldest month, and July being the warmest month. The area also experiences low atmospheric pressure and high altitude, resulting in lower oxygen levels and stronger ultraviolet radiation. The annual precipitation ranges from 400 to 900 mm (16 to 35 in), with most of it concentrated in the months from May to September. The combination of high altitude and cold climate makes the region a unique and challenging environment.
