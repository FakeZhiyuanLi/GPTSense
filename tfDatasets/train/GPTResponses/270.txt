1. Birth and infancy: The beginning of life as a newborn and learning basic survival skills.2. Childhood: Learning, playing, and socializing with others. Developing social, emotional, and cognitive intelligence.3. Adolescence: Transitioning into teenage years and exploring personal identity. Developing independence and decision-making skills.4. Adulthood: Entering the workforce, building relationships, and starting families. Developing career aspirations, personal interests, and hobbies.5. Middle age: Raising children, contributing to society, and achieving goals. Dealing with the challenges of aging, such as health concerns.6. Old age: Retiring from work and reflecting on life, spending time with family and friends, and coming to terms with mortality.
