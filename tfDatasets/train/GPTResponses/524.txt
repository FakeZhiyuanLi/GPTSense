The Buddha taught that to end suffering, one must give up attachment and craving. He famously spoke of the Four Noble Truths, which state:1. Suffering exists2. Suffering arises from craving and attachment3. Suffering ceases when craving and attachment are abandoned4. The Noble Eightfold Path is the way to end craving and attachment and thus end suffering.In short, the Buddha taught that to end suffering, one must give up attachment to desires and cravings and follow the Noble Eightfold Path to cultivate mindfulness, ethical conduct, and wisdom.
