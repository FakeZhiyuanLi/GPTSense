Robbery is the act of taking property by force or threat of force from an individual or a place. Piracy is the act of stealing, attacking or hijacking ships at sea or aircraft in the air. Robbery typically occurs on land and involves taking something from a person or property while piracy usually involves a maritime setting and the taking of a vessel or its cargo. Additionally, piracy is considered an international crime and can have significant political and economic ramifications, while robbery is generally considered a local crime.
