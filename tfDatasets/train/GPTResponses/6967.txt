Child safety and public library software are used to filter and restrict access to inappropriate or unsafe content for children, including:1. Adult content - including pornography, nudity, and graphic violence2. Hate speech and discriminatory content3. Illegal activities, such as hacking or piracy4. Social media and messaging apps5. Gambling and other addictive activities6. Malware and viruses7. Phishing and other online scams8. Inappropriate language or discussion forumsThese software are designed to protect children from exposure to harmful content and promote safe and responsible internet use. They also provide parents and librarians with control and monitoring tools to ensure their children's safety online.
