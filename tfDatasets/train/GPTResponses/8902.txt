The Guardian Building is an example of Art Deco Style, a popular aesthetic design style during the 1920s and 1930s. Its exterior is decorated with intricate geometric patterns, figurative sculptures, and ornate metalwork, while its interior features colorful marble and glass, intricate plasterwork, and decorative lighting fixtures. Overall, the building is a masterpiece of Art Deco architecture and a one-of-a-kind landmark in Detroit, Michigan.
