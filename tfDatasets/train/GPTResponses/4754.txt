Admiral Sir Mark Stanhope is a retired senior officer of the Royal Navy, who served as the First Sea Lord and Chief of Naval Staff from 2009 to 2013. During his naval career, he commanded several vessels and held various leadership positions including Commander-in-Chief Fleet and Deputy Supreme Allied Commander Europe. He was appointed a Knight Commander of the Order of the Bath in 2008 and currently holds several non-executive roles in the private and public sector.
