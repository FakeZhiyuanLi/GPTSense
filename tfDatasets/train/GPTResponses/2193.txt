After Louis the Pious, Italy was ruled by several different rulers, including:1. Lothair I, who inherited Italy from Louis the Pious but later had to share it with his brothers after their father's death.2. Louis II, who succeeded his father, Lothair I, as the King of Italy.3. Charles the Bald, who became King of Italy after his brother, Louis II, died.4. Berengar I, who was one of the last Lombard kings of Italy and ruled from 888 to 924.5. Hugh of Arles, who was the last King of Italy before the country was united under the Holy Roman Empire.
