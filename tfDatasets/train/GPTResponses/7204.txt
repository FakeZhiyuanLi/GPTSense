The most famous cubist architecture building is the Villa Savoye in Poissy, France, designed by Le Corbusier in the late 1920s. It is considered a masterpiece of modern architecture and a prime example of the International Style. The building features a clean and simple geometric design, with square and rectangular shapes, and an emphasis on function and efficiency. The Villa Savoye has inspired many architects and designers over the years and remains a popular tourist attraction in France.
