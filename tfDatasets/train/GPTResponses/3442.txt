There are many archipelagos that lie off the mainland of different continents. Some of the most known ones are:- The Galapagos Islands off the coast of Ecuador in South America- The Hawaiian Islands off the coast of the United States in North America- The British Isles off the coast of Europe- The Balearic Islands off the coast of Spain in Europe- The Ionian Islands off the coast of Greece in Europe- The Canary Islands off the coast of Morocco in Africa- The Philippines off the coast of Southeast Asia
