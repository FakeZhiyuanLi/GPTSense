Metadata can contain a wide range of information that describes different aspects of a digital file. Some examples of metadata that may be included in a file's properties or tags are:- Album title- Genre- Release year- Composer- Duration- File format and encoding parameters- Bit rate and sampling rate- Lyrics or textual notes- Cover art or other visual media- Copyright information and license terms- Language and region settings- User-defined tags and comments.In addition, metadata can also be used for technical purposes, such as identifying the software used to create or process the file, tracking its history or version status, or embedding security or authentication measures.
