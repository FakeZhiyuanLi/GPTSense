The most notable rock paintings can be found in various locations around the world, including:- Altamira Cave in Spain- Lascaux Cave in France- Serra da Capivara National Park in Brazil- Tsodilo Hills in Botswana- Kakadu National Park in Australia- Tassili n'Ajjer in Algeria- Chauvet-Pont-d'Arc Cave in France- Bhimbetka rock shelters in India- Cueva de las Manos in Argentina.
