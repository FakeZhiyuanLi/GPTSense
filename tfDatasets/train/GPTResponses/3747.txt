Emotions can cause a variety of changes, including:1. Mood swings: Emotions can cause rapid changes in mood, making a person feel happy one moment and sad the next.2. Changes in behavior: Emotions can affect behavior, causing a person to act differently than they normally would. For example, someone who is angry may become more aggressive or defensive.3. Changes in perception: Emotions can alter a person's perception of reality, making something seem more positive or negative than it actually is.4. Changes in memory: Emotions can affect memory, making it easier or harder to remember certain events or details.5. Changes in physical sensations: Emotions can cause physical sensations such as sweating, shaking, racing heart, or butterflies in the stomach.6. Changes in communication: Emotions can impact a person's ability to communicate effectively, causing them to speak louder, faster, or slower than usual.
