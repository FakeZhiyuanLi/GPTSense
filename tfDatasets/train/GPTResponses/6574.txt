Traditionally, on Fat Tuesday, people eat foods that are rich in fats, sugar, and eggs since the day before the Lent starts, and they need to use up their stores of these ingredients. Some popular Fat Tuesday foods include:1. Pancakes2. Doughnuts3. King Cake4. Paczki5. Beignets6. Fasnachts7. Mardi Gras Jambalaya8. Gumbo9. Fried Chicken10. Shrimp and gritsThese dishes are celebrated around the world, and each culture celebrates the day differently.
