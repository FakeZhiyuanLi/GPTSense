The most notable SAM (Surface-to-Air Missile) that can detect a stealth target is the Russian S-400 Triumf. The S-400 has advanced radar systems that can detect and track stealth aircraft, and it is known for its long-range and high accuracy. The S-400 has been deployed by the Russian military in Syria to protect its assets from potential threats from other countries. It is considered one of the most advanced SAM systems in the world.
