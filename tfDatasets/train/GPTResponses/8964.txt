Barrow bikes are typically used for transportation of goods or heavy objects in urban or rural areas. They are an eco-friendly, low-cost alternative to using motor vehicles and can be used to transport a range of items, from groceries to construction materials. They are also commonly used in outdoor events, such as festivals and markets, to transport goods and supplies. Some people also use barrow bikes for exercise or leisure activities, such as cycling around the city with a friend or a pet.
