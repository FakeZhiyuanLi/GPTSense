Emails enable the launching of various communication activities such as sending messages, files, documents, images, videos, and audio messages to one or multiple recipients at the same time. Additionally, emails support a wide range of business activities, including marketing, sales, customer support, and internal communication. Emails also facilitate the process of sharing information, coordinating tasks, scheduling meetings, and collaborating with others. Finally, emails provide a convenient and professional way to maintain ongoing communication with colleagues, clients, customers, and other stakeholders.
