Artificial intelligence (AI) technology has become more widely available and affordable in recent years. With the development of cloud computing and machine learning algorithms, businesses and individuals can now access AI technology to automate tasks, analyze data, and enhance decision-making processes. In addition, the rise of smart home devices and personal assistants such as Amazon's Alexa and Google Assistant have made AI technology accessible to consumers in their everyday lives. Other technologies that have become more widely available and affordable include virtual reality (VR), 3D printing, and renewable energy technologies such as solar panels and wind turbines.
