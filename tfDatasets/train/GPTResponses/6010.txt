Postcode 3000 is a strategic plan launched by the City of Melbourne in 1991. Its aim was to regenerate the central business district (CBD) and surrounding areas by attracting new residents, businesses, and investment. The plan sought to create a vibrant, mixed-use urban environment that incorporated residential, commercial, and cultural amenities.Melbourne 2030 is a strategic plan launched by the Victorian government in 2002. Its aim was to manage the growth and development of Greater Melbourne over the next 30 years. The plan sought to accommodate increasing population growth and demand for housing, jobs, and services by encouraging infill development in designated activity centers and transport corridors. It also aimed to protect green wedges and other natural areas from development pressures.In summary, these plans aimed to improve the liveability and sustainability of Melbourne by promoting urban renewal and strategic development that would accommodate growth and protect valuable environmental assets.
