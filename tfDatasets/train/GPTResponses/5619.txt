The most commonly used antihypertensive drug is a thiazide diuretic, such as hydrochlorothiazide. Other commonly used antihypertensive drugs include ACE inhibitors, angiotensin receptor blockers (ARBs), beta-blockers, calcium channel blockers, and aldosterone antagonists. The choice of drug and combination of drugs depends on various factors such as age, race, comorbidities, and previous medication history.
