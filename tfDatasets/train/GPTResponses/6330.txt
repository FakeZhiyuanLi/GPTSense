Poultry and poultry products are rich in various essential nutrients that are important for human health. These nutrients include:1. Protein: Poultry and poultry products are a rich source of high-quality protein, which is essential for building and repairing tissues in the body.2. Vitamins: Poultry and poultry products are rich in various vitamins, including vitamin B6, vitamin B12, niacin, and riboflavin. Fats: Poultry and poultry products contain both saturated and unsaturated fats. While the saturated fats should be consumed in moderation, the unsaturated fats are heart-healthy and can help reduce the risk of heart diseases.5. Low in carbohydrates: Poultry and poultry products have little to no carbohydrates, making them an excellent source of high protein for people on low-carbohydrate diets.Overall, poultry and poultry products are a nutritious source of protein, vitamins, minerals, and healthy fats that can provide numerous health benefits to humans.
