The term given to the head of music may vary depending on the organization or institution. Some common terms include:1. Director of Music: This term is often used for the head of music in academic institutions, such as universities or colleges.2. Music Director: This term is commonly used for the head of music in performing arts organizations, such as orchestras or choirs.3. Head of Music: This term is a general title that may be used in various contexts to refer to the person in charge of the music department.4. Music Supervisor: This term may be used in the film and television industry to refer to the person in charge of selecting and/or creating music for a production.5. Music Coordinator: This term may be used in the music industry to refer to someone who is responsible for coordinating various aspects of music production, such as recording sessions or tours.
