The parts of a trophy include:1. Base: The bottom of the trophy that supports the rest of the structure2. Stem: A central pillar or column that connects the base to the top of the trophy3. Cup or Bowl: The main part of the trophy that usually can hold something, such as liquid or a ball4. Handles: A pair of handles or one handle attached to the body of the trophy5. Engraving plate: A metal plate attached to the base or stem of the trophy, which is engraved with the winner's name or other information6. Finial or Topper: A decorative element that sits on top of the trophy or replaces the cup or bowl. It often portrays the sport or activity for which the trophy is awarded.
