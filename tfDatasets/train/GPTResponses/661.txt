In the Democratic Republic of the Congo, the forms of transportation available include:1. Air travel - with numerous domestic airlines operating within the country, flying domestically within the Congo is relatively easy.2. Road transport - both public and private road transport are available in Congo including taxis and buses. However, many roads are in poor condition hence making transportation relatively difficult.3. Water transport - with the Congo river, the second-longest river in Africa, water transport is a major means of transportation especially in transporting goods.4. Rail transport - while not as popular, there are rail services that operate within the Congo.5. Motorcycles (boda-boda)- they are a common form of transportation in Congo's more remote villages and towns.
