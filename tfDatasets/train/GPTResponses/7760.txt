Greek philosophers were trying to answer a wide range of questions related to various aspects of life such as ethics, politics, metaphysics, epistemology, and aesthetics. Some of the core questions that they attempted to answer include:1. What is the nature of reality?2. What is the ultimate substance that everything is made of?3. What is the origin of the universe and how did it come into being?4. What is the purpose of life?5. What is the nature of knowledge?6. What is the best form of government?7. What is the meaning of morality and how should people behave?8. What is the nature of the human soul?
