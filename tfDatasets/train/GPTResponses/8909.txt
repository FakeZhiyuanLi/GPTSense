Some of the areas of Detroit that contain high rises include:1. Downtown Detroit - contains many office buildings and apartment buildings with high rises.2. Midtown Detroit - has several high-rise apartment buildings as well as office buildings.3. New Center - located just north of the Midtown area and contains several high-rise office buildings.4. Greektown - contains several high-rise hotels and residential buildings.5. Riverfront - located along the Detroit River, this area contains several high-rise residential buildings and hotels.Overall, many of Detroit's high-rises are located in the downtown and midtown areas, as well as along the riverfront.
