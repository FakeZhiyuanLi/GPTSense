In the Roman Catholic practice of dulia, various saints are venerated, including but not limited to:1. Mary, the mother of Jesus2. St. Joseph, the husband of Mary3. St. Peter, the first Pope4. St. Paul, the apostle5. St. Francis of Assisi, the founder of the Franciscan Order6. St. Therese of Lisieux, the "Little Flower"7. St. Teresa of Avila, the Carmelite mystic and reformer8. St. Dominic, the founder of the Dominican Order9. St. Ignatius of Loyola, the founder of the Jesuits10. St. Catherine of Siena, the medieval mystic and Doctor of the Church.
