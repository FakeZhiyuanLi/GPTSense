Rome Laboratory is a research laboratory of the United States Air Force located in Rome, New York. It is also known as the Air Force Research Laboratory Information Directorate. The laboratory conducts research and development in the areas of Information Technology, Cybersecurity, Command and Control Systems, and Intelligence, Surveillance, and Reconnaissance technologies. The Rome Laboratory works with industry, academia, and other government agencies to develop innovative solutions that support the U.S. Air Force and national security.
