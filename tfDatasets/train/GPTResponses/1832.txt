There were many cathedrals built in the 15th century, so it depends on which one you are referring to. Some examples are:- Seville Cathedral (also known as Cathedral of Saint Mary of the See) in Spain, completed in 1507- Milan Cathedral (also known as the Duomo) in Italy, completed in 1499- St. Peter's Basilica in Vatican City, Rome, Italy, which began construction in 1506 and was completed in the 17th century- Westminster Abbey in London, England, which was rebuilt in the 15th century and has been a cathedral at times throughout its history.
