Minor league hockey and baseball teams in New Haven have played in the following currently defunct venues throughout the latter half of the 20th century:1. New Haven Arena - This arena was located on Grove Street, and it was home to several minor league hockey teams, including the New Haven Blades and the New Haven Nighthawks.2. Yale Field - This stadium was situated on Central Avenue and was primarily used for baseball. It was the home field of the New Haven Ravens, a minor league baseball team that played in the Eastern League.3. Veterans Memorial Coliseum - This facility, located on Orange Street, was home to the New Haven Senators, a minor league hockey team that played in the American Hockey League.4. West Haven Veterans Memorial Skating Rink - This ice rink was located in neighboring West Haven and was the former home of the New Haven Knights, a minor league hockey team that played in the Eastern Hockey League.
