There have been many exhibitions held around the world that have shown agricultural and industrial progress. However, one of the most significant was the Great Exhibition of 1851, held in London, England. This exhibition was the first international exposition of its kind and showcased innovations in industry and agriculture from across the globe. It featured over 100,000 items, including farming equipment, machinery, textiles, and other products that demonstrated the advancements of the Industrial Revolution. The exhibition attracted more than six million visitors and had a significant impact on the development of science, technology, and industry in the years that followed.
