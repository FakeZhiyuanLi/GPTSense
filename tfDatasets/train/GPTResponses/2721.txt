The term "transient" can refer to a variety of things depending on the context. Here are some possible accompanying factors:1. Transient person/homeless person: lack of permanent housing, limited access to basic necessities such as food, water, and healthcare, social isolation, prejudice and discrimination.2. Transient ischemic attack (TIA): temporary loss or reduction of blood flow to the brain causing stroke-like symptoms such as weakness, dizziness, slurred speech risk factors include high blood pressure, high cholesterol, smoking, and diabetes.
