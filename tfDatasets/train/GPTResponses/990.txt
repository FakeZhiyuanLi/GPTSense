Clinical immunology also concerns itself with:1. Autoimmunity: The study of conditions where the immune system attacks the body's own cells and tissues.2. Immunodeficiency disorders: The study of conditions where the immune system is unable to properly function, resulting in an increased susceptibility to infections.3. Immune-mediated diseases: The study of diseases that are caused or worsened by the immune system, such as allergies, asthma, and inflammatory bowel disease.4. Immunotherapy: The study of treatments that involve manipulating the immune system to treat diseases, such as cancer immunotherapy.5. Transplantation immunology: The study of the immune system's response to transplant procedures, including rejection and immune suppression therapies.6. Immunogenetics: The study of genetic factors that influence the immune system's function and potential for diseases.
