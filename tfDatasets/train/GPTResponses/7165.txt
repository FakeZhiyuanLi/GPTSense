Organophosphates and carbamates can cause a wide range of symptoms, including:1. Nausea and vomiting2. Headache3. Dizziness4. Difficulty breathing5. Muscle weakness6. Twitching or tremors7. Seizures8. Loss of consciousness9. Excessive sweating or salivation10. Diarrhea or abdominal crampsThe severity of symptoms varies depending on the type and amount of pesticide exposure, as well as individual factors such as age and overall health. In severe cases, exposure to these chemicals can be life-threatening.
