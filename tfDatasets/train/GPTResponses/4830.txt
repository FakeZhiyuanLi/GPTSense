The Charles de Gaulle aircraft carrier carries various missile types, including:1. Aster surface-to-air missiles: to defend against airborne threats.2. Exocet anti-ship missiles: to attack enemy ships or submarines.3. Scalp/Storm Shadow cruise missiles: to strike strategic land targets.4. Mica air-to-air missiles: to engage enemy aircraft.5. ASMP-A nuclear missiles: for strategic deterrence against potential threats.
