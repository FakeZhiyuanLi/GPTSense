Copper is supplied in a variety of forms including:1. Copper wire - often used in electrical applications2. Copper tube - used in plumbing and HVAC systems3. Copper sheet - used for roofing, flashing, and other construction purposes4. Copper bars and rods - used in engineering and manufacturing applications5. Copper alloy products - including brass and bronze, used in a variety of applications.Copper can be purchased from metal suppliers, manufacturers, and distributors in various shapes and sizes depending on the application. It can also be recycled from scrap metal.
