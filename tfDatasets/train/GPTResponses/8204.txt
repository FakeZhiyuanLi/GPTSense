The idea of the image of God allows man to understand that he has inherent value and worth, as he was created in the image of God. This understanding inspires man to strive for righteousness, justice, and love towards God and others. It also gives man a sense of purpose and meaning in life, as he knows that he was created for a specific reason and has a significant role to play in the world. Overall, the idea of the image of God empowers man to live a meaningful and fulfilling life according to the principles of his Creator.
