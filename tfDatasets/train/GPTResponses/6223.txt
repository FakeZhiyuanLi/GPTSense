Many Sanskrit texts have survived in India, especially in the southern region including Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, and Kerala. Some of the major centers for Sanskrit learning and preservation include Varanasi, Ujjain, Haridwar, and Pune. The manuscripts have been preserved in various libraries, including the National Archives of India and the libraries of universities like Banaras Hindu University and Jawaharlal Nehru University. Additionally, many texts have been passed down orally through generations of pandits and scholars.
