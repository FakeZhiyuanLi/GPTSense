Some of the factors that have made videoconferencing accessible to many are:1. Increase in internet bandwidth and speed2. Advancements in video compression technology3. Adoption of videoconferencing by businesses, schools, and healthcare providers6. The need to connect with people remotely due to geographical distance or safety concerns.
