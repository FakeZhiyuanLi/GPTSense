MP3 files commonly contain the following metadata:1. Title - The name of the song or piece of music.2. Artist - The name of the artist or performers.3. Album - The album the song or piece of music comes from.4. Track number - The track number of the song on the album.5. Genre - The type of music, such as rock, pop, or classical.6. Year - The year the song or music was released.7. Composer - The name of the composer of the piece of music.8. Duration - The length of the song or piece of music.9. Bitrate - The quality of the sound, measured in kilobits per second (kbps).10. Comment - A place for users to add their own comments or notes about the song or music.
