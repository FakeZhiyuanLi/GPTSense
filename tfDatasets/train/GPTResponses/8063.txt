The Bermudian dollar (BMD) is officially pegged to the United States dollar (USD) at a rate of 1:1. This means that the Bermudian dollar can be used interchangeably with the US dollar in Bermuda and other countries that accept US dollars. However, it is important to note that while the Bermudian dollar and US dollar have the same value in Bermuda, they may have different exchange rates in other countries.
