Various structures and facilities can be built around naval installations, depending on their purpose and location. Here are some examples:1. Fortress walls and defensive structures to protect the naval base from enemy attacks.2. Quays and piers for ships to dock and unload supplies and equipment.3. Dry docks and repair facilities to maintain and repair ships.4. Warehouses and storage facilities to house supplies, weapons, and equipment.5. Housing and barracks for the naval personnel and their families.6. Hospitals and medical facilities to provide healthcare services.7. Training centers and classrooms for educating and training naval personnel.8. Research and development centers for new technologies and innovations.9. Communication and surveillance systems to monitor and control naval operations.10. Fueling and refueling stations for ships and aircraft.
