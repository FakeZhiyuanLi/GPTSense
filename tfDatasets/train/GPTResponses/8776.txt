Germany and France switched control of Alsace-Lorraine several times throughout history:1. The region was part of the Holy Roman Empire until the 17th century.2. In 1871, Germany annexed Alsace-Lorraine from France after winning the Franco-Prussian War.3. After World War I, Alsace-Lorraine was returned to France as part of the Treaty of Versailles.4. During World War II, Germany reoccupied Alsace-Lorraine.5. After the war, Alsace-Lorraine was returned to France again.Therefore, Germany and France switched control of Alsace-Lorraine at least three times.
