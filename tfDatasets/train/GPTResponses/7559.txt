The 5th Dalai Lama and his intimates established the Ganden Phodrang government which unified Tibet for the first time and strengthened Tibetan Buddhism. They also completed the construction of the Potala Palace, which became the official residence of the Dalai Lamas. Additionally, the 5th Dalai Lama established diplomatic relations with neighboring countries and provided patronage to various artists, scholars, and writers resulting in a flourishing of Tibetan culture.
