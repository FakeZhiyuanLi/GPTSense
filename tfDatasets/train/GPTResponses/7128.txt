There are several entities that can take steps to prevent infringement:1. Intellectual property holders - They can take measures such as filing patents, trademarks, or copyrights to protect their creations from being copied or used without permission.2. Law enforcement agencies - They can investigate and prosecute infringers, seize counterfeit products, and enforce intellectual property laws.3. Internet service providers (ISPs) - They can implement measures like website blocking, content filtering, and notice-and-takedown systems to prevent copyright infringement on their platforms.4. Consumer advocacy groups - They can raise public awareness about the importance of intellectual property rights and educate consumers about legal alternatives to piracy.5. Courts - They can issue injunctions, damages, and other legal remedies to prevent and punish infringement.
