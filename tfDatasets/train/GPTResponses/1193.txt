There have been many members of American diplomatic and military circles throughout history. Eisenhower: a five-star general in the US Army who later served as the 34th President of the United States- Colin Powell: a retired four-star general in the US Army who served as Chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff and Secretary of State- Condoleezza Rice: a former Secretary of State who also served as National Security Advisor under President George W. Bush- John F. Kennedy: a former President of the United States who served in the US Navy during World War IIThese are just a few examples there have been countless others who have served in important roles within American diplomatic and military circles.
