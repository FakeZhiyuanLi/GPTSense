The Saxon Duchies consisted of three main duchies:1. Duchy of Saxony: This was the original duchy founded by Charlemagne that included modern-day Saxony-Anhalt, Lower Saxony, and Hamburg.2. Duchy of Westphalia: This duchy was established in the 10th century and included modern-day North Rhine-Westphalia.3. Duchy of Holstein: This duchy was established in the 12th century and included modern-day Schleswig-Holstein.
