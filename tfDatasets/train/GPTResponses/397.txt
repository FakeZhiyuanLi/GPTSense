There are many cities located near ferry terminals around the world, so it's difficult to pinpoint just one. Here are a few examples:- Seattle, Washington, USA: The Seattle ferry terminal is located in the downtown area, near Pike Place Market and other tourist attractions.- Sydney, Australia: The Circular Quay ferry terminal is located in the heart of the city, offering views of the Opera House and Harbour Bridge.- Hong Kong, China: The Central Ferry Piers are located in the financial district, offering easy access to other areas of the city via water taxis.- Amsterdam, Netherlands: The Amsterdam Ferry Terminal is located at the Amsterdam Central Station, providing connections to other parts of the city and beyond via waterways.
