Apart from traditional schools where Sanskrit is commonly taught, it is also taught in:1. Ayurvedic Colleges: Sanskrit is an integral part of Ayurveda, and students pursuing Ayurveda also learn Sanskrit language.2. Yoga Institutes: Many yoga institutes offer courses in Sanskrit to help students understand the traditional texts and teachings of yoga.3. Universities: Several universities across the world offer Sanskrit courses as a part of their curriculum. They offer undergraduate and postgraduate courses in Sanskrit and related fields.4. Independent Language Institutes: Many independent language institutes offer Sanskrit courses for those who are interested in learning the language.5. International Schools: Some international schools also offer Sanskrit as an optional subject in their curriculum.6. Online Learning Platforms: With the increasing popularity of online learning, many e-learning platforms offer Sanskrit language courses, making it accessible to learners across the world.
