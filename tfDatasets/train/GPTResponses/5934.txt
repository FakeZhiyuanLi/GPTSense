Sanskrit can be used for various applications apart from being a language of religions:1. Literature: Sanskrit language has a rich literary tradition with numerous literary works in various genres such as poetry, drama, philosophy, and science.2. Education: Sanskrit is considered as the language of ancient Indian education system. Sanskrit texts can still be found in many Indian schools and universities.3. Science: Many scientific texts in fields like medicine, astronomy, mathematics, etc., have been written in Sanskrit. The language has its own unique symbols and terminologies that make it a valuable resource for academic research.4. Yoga and meditation: Many practices and concepts in Yoga and meditation are based on Sanskrit literature. Sanskrit mantras and chants are still used in many spiritual practices.5. Cultural heritage: Sanskrit is considered as one of the oldest languages in the world and has played a significant role in the development of Indian culture and tradition. It holds great value as a part of cultural heritage and Indian identity.
