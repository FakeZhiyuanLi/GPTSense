Copper has been used for a variety of purposes since ancient times, including:1. Tools: Copper was one of the first metals to be used in tools because of its malleability, durability and ability to conduct heat.2. Weapons: Copper was also used to make weapons such as swords, daggers, and spearheads.3. Jewelry: Copper was used to make jewelry because of its lustrous appearance and its ability to be shaped into intricate designs.4. Coins: Many ancient civilizations used copper to mint coins.5. Buildings: Copper was also used in construction because of its durability and resistance to corrosion.6. Medicine: Copper was used in ancient times as a form of medicine because of its antibacterial properties.7. Water pipes: Copper was used to make water pipes because of its durability and ability to resist corrosion.
