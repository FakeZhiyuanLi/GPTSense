There have been many ancient capitals throughout China's history, but the most commonly recognized are 6:1. Xian (Chang'an) - this was the capital of many dynasties, including the Qin, Han, Sui, and Tang dynasties.2. Luoyang - this was the capital of many dynasties, including the Eastern Zhou, Han, and Wei dynasties.3. Nanjing - this was the capital of the Ming dynasty.4. Beijing - this was the capital of the Jin, Yuan, Ming, and Qing dynasties.5. Kaifeng - this was the capital of the Northern Song dynasty.6. Anyang - this was the capital of the Shang dynasty.
