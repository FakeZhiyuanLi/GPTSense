Florida has the largest collection of manatees in the world. The state is home to more than 6,000 manatees, which is about half the global population of this marine mammal species. Manatees are a protected species in Florida and are found in the state's warm coastal waters, rivers, and estuaries. People can view these gentle giants in their natural habitat by taking guided tours or visiting one of the state's many manatee observation sites.
