A wrestler can win in modern times by:1. Pinning their opponent's shoulders to the mat for a count of 3.2. Making their opponent submit by forcing them to tap out or verbally surrender.3. Scoring more points than their opponent by executing takedowns, escapes, and reversals.4. Winning by disqualification, if their opponent violates the rules of the match.5. Winning by forfeit, if their opponent fails to show up or is unable to compete.6. Winning by decision, if at the end of the match both wrestlers have an equal number of points, the wrestler with the most near falls or highest scoring moves will be declared the winner.7. Winning by technical fall, if a wrestler has a 15-point lead over their opponent, the match is ended and the wrestler with the lead is declared the winner.
