Environmentalists want a range of measures enacted to protect the environment and reduce the negative impacts of human activities on the planet. This includes:1. Improving regulations and enforcement of laws to reduce pollution and protect natural habitats.2. Promoting renewable energy sources and phasing out fossil fuels.3. Supporting sustainable agriculture and reducing food waste.4. Protecting natural resources, such as water and forests.5. Fighting climate change through emissions reduction and carbon pricing.6. Encouraging public transportation and reducing car dependence.7. Supporting conservation efforts for endangered species and protecting biodiversity.8. Promoting sustainable urban planning and reducing urban sprawl.9. Educating the public on environmental issues and encouraging action.Overall, environmentalists want policies that prioritize the health of the planet and its inhabitants, both now and in the future.
