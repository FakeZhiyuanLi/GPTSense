The National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) was primarily a base for conducting research, development, and operations related to space exploration and aeronautics. NASA's mission was to drive advances in science, technology, aeronautics, and space exploration that improve human understanding of Earth and the universe. The agency operates various facilities, including centers, laboratories, and test facilities, where scientists, engineers, and researchers work on projects ranging from space missions, spacecraft design, launch vehicles, and exoplanet exploration, among others.
