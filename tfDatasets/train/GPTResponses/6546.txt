Some of the best-known traditions were first recorded in ancient civilizations such as Egypt, Greece, Rome, China, India, and Mesopotamia. For example, the tradition of Christmas was first recorded in Rome in the 4th century AD, while the celebration of Diwali originated in ancient India over 2,500 years ago. The art of Chinese calligraphy was first recorded in China over 2,000 years ago, and the Olympic Games were first recorded in ancient Greece in 776 BC.
