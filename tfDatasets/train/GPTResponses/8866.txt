Utrecht's location allowed for it to become a major commercial and transportation hub. It is located near the center of the Netherlands, on the banks of the Rhine River, which allowed for easy transportation of goods and people. Utrecht also sits at the intersection of several major highways and rail lines, making it an important transportation hub for both national and international travel. Additionally, its central location made it a desirable location for political and religious institutions, leading to the development of numerous universities and churches in the city.
