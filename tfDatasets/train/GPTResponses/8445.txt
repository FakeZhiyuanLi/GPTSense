Many old master paintings from the Germanic Rhenish territories until 1681 are held in various museums and art galleries across Europe, including Germany, France, and the Netherlands. Some notable museums and galleries that hold these paintings include:1. The Städel Museum, Frankfurt2. The Wallraf-Richartz Museum, Cologne3. The Louvre Museum, Paris4. The National Gallery, London5. The Rijksmuseum, Amsterdam6. The Alte Pinakothek, Munich7. The Gemäldegalerie, BerlinThere are also many private collections across Europe that hold these paintings.
