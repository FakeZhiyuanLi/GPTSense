Light carriers had several advantages over escort carriers:1. Speed: Light carriers were faster and more maneuverable than escort carriers, allowing them to keep up with faster fleet carriers and quickly respond to changing battle conditions.2. Armament: Light carriers were equipped with more powerful weapons, including larger and more effective aircraft, AA guns, and torpedoes. This made them more effective in combat situations.3. Sturdiness: Light carriers were built with stronger hulls and engines, making them harder to damage and more reliable in combat.4. Versatility: Light carriers had more flexible mission capabilities, as they were able to support a wider range of operations such as air support, reconnaissance, and anti-submarine warfare.Overall, light carriers were able to provide more effective support to combat operations than escort carriers, allowing them to play a more instrumental role in naval combat.
