The faith in and veneration of Mahayana are stated in various sutras, including:1. Lotus Sutra (Saddharma Pundarika Sutra)2. Heart Sutra (Prajnaparamita Hridaya Sutra)3. Diamond Sutra (Vajracchedika Prajnaparamita Sutra)4. Avatamsaka Sutra (Flower Garland Sutra)5. Amitabha Sutra (Infinite Light Sutra)6. Mahaparinirvana Sutra (Great Nirvana Sutra)7. Vimalakirti Nirdesa Sutra (Vimalakirti Sutra)8. Surangama Sutra (Leng Yen Ching)These sutras are considered the core texts of Mahayana Buddhism and are used by Mahayana practitioners for meditation, prayers, and spiritual contemplation. They emphasize the compassionate nature of the bodhisattva path and the aspiration to attain enlightenment for the welfare of all sentient beings.
