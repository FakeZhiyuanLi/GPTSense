The onboard microphones in cars with built-in voice recognition features can be used for the following purposes:1. Voice commands - Drivers can use these microphones to give voice commands to the car's entertainment, communication, and navigation systems.2. Phone calls - The microphones can be used for hands-free phone calls, allowing drivers to make and receive calls without taking their hands off the wheel.3. Voice-to-text dictation - Some vehicles allow drivers to dictate text messages or emails using the microphones.4. Emergency calls - The microphones can be used to make emergency calls in case of accidents or other emergencies.5. Noise cancellation - The microphones can also be used to cancel out background noise and improve the clarity of voice commands and phone calls.
