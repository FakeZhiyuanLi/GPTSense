In the world of sports, "friendlies" refer to non-competitive matches or games played between teams as part of their training, practice, or exhibition purposes.To honor a player in the UK, a testimonial match is often held. This is a game that is played for the benefit of a player who has given a long service to a particular club or team. The match is usually organized at the end of the player's career, and its purpose is to give fans and teammates a chance to honor the player for their dedication and achievements. The proceeds from the match typically go to the player or a charity of their choice.
