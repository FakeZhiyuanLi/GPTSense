There have been many geologists and scientists who have studied the rock formations of the Alps over the years. Some notable figures include:- Horace-Bénédict de Saussure (1740-1799): a Swiss geologist, physicist, and Alpine explorer who made valuable observations and analyses of the rocks and glaciers in the Alps.- Louis Agassiz (1807-1873): a Swiss-American geologist and paleontologist who conducted extensive studies of the glacial deposits and rock formations in the Alps.- Arnold Fanck (1889-1974): a German geologist and filmmaker who specialized in studying the geological features of mountain regions, particularly the Alps.- Eduard Suess (1831-1914): an Austrian geologist who conducted extensive studies and research on the tectonic and geological history of the Alps.
