Railway is not a commonly used mode of transportation in the Himachal Pradesh state of India. The state is famous for its scenic beauty, snow-clad mountains, trekking trails, adventure sports, and rich cultural heritage. Some of the famous attractions in Himachal Pradesh include Shimla, Manali, Dharamshala, McLeod Ganj, Dalhousie, Kullu, Kasauli, and Kinnaur.
