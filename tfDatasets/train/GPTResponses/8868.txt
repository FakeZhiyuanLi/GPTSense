In Cork, other types of public transportation include:1. Trains- The city has a main train station, Kent Station, which operates national and intercity services.2. Trams- The Luas tram system operates in and around the city, connecting it to neighboring towns and villages.3. Taxis- Taxis can be hailed on the street or booked via phone or app, making them a convenient mode of public transportation.4. Ferries- Boats operate services from the city to locations such as Spike Island and Cobh, offering scenic views of Cork Harbor.5. Bicycles- Cork has a public bike scheme, which allows users to rent bikes for short trips around the city.
