Kangra and Dharamshala are famous for their scenic beauty, temples, and spiritual centers. Kangra is known for the Kangra Fort, Brajeshwari Temple, and Masroor Rock Cut Temple. Dharamshala is famous for the Dalai Lama temple and his residence, McLeod Ganj, Bhagsu Nag, and the Indrahar Pass trek. Both places are renowned for their picturesque landscapes, including snow-capped mountains, lush green forests, and glistening streams. They are also popular for their handicrafts, including exquisite miniature paintings, shawls, and jewellery.
