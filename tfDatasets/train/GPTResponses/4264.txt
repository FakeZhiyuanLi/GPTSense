The Kinect significantly boosted sales of the Xbox 360 console. The device was released in November 2010 and by January 2011, more than 8 million units had been sold. In the first two months of its release, Kinect sold at an average rate of 133,333 units per day. The popularity of Kinect helped the Xbox 360 to outsell the PlayStation 3 and the Nintendo Wii in the holiday season of 2010. Kinect also attracted a new audience to the Xbox 360, including families and casual gamers who were interested in its motion sensing features.
