There are several types of buildings that attract tourists to Nanjing, including:1. Historical architecture: Nanjing has a rich history spanning more than 2,000 years, and many of its ancient structures such as the City Wall, the Confucius Temple, and the Ming Xiaoling Mausoleum are top attractions that showcase its architectural heritage.2. Modern skyscrapers: Nanjing is also home to several modern skyscrapers, including Zifeng Tower, Jinling Hotel, and the Nanjing International Youth Cultural Centre, which offer panoramic views of the city and its surroundings.3. Museums and cultural institutions: The Nanjing Museum, the Presidential Palace, and the Nanjing Massacre Memorial Hall are just a few examples of museums and cultural institutions that attract tourists interested in learning more about the city's past and present.4. Religious sites: Nanjing is renowned for its numerous temples and pagodas, such as the Jiming Temple, Qinhuai River Scenic Belt, and the Linggu Temple, which offer a peaceful retreat from the hustle and bustle of the city.5. Gardens and parks: Nanjing is also home to several beautiful parks and gardens, including the Xuanwu Lake Park, the Mochou Lake Park, and the Purple Mountain Scenic Area, which offer visitors a chance to enjoy the natural beauty of the city.
