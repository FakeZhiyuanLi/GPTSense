Federal agencies are required to use the categories of race and ethnicity established by the Office of Management and Budget (OMB), which include:- American Indian or Alaska Native- Asian- Black or African American- Native Hawaiian or Other Pacific Islander- White- Two or more races (e.g. white and black or Asian and Pacific Islander)In addition to these race categories, federal agencies are required to collect data on ethnicity, which includes:- Hispanic or Latino- Not Hispanic or Latino
