identityGender identity refers to an individual's internal sense of their gender, which may be male, female, non-binary, or another gender identity. Ethnic identity refers to an individual's sense of belonging to a particular ethnic group, including shared cultural practices, language, and traditions. Occupational identity refers to an individual's identification with a particular occupation or field of work, including their skills, interests, and values related to their work. These are all aspects of an individual's identity that can influence how they see themselves and interact with the world around them.
