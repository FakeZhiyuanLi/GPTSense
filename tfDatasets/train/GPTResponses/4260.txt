According to TechRadar, the Xbox 360's influence went beyond gaming and media distribution due to its user-friendly interface, robust online network capabilities, and its ability to function as a home entertainment hub. It also offered features such as social networking, video calling, and access to streaming apps like Netflix and Hulu, which expanded the console's functionality beyond gaming. Additionally, the 360's development of Kinect, a motion-sensing device, paved the way for future advances in gesture-based gaming and computing.
