Registered Dietitians (RD) and Nutritionists offer dietary advice on the role of nutrition in chronic diseases. They are trained and qualified professionals who specialize in nutrition and dietary counseling. They work in various healthcare settings, including hospitals, clinics, private practice, and community health centers. They provide evidence-based nutritional guidance to help individuals manage and prevent chronic diseases such as obesity, diabetes, hypertension, cardiovascular diseases, and cancer. They help individuals develop personalized nutrition plans and make dietary changes to improve their health outcomes.
