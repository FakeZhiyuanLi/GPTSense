Yes, several clubs have won both the domestic league and cup competitions in the same season. This is known as a domestic double. Some examples include:- Manchester United in the 1993-1994 season, winning both the English Premier League and the FA Cup.- Barcelona in the 2008-2009 season, winning both La Liga and the Copa del Rey.- Bayern Munich in the 2018-2019 season, winning both the Bundesliga and the DFB-Pokal.- Chelsea in the 2020-2021 season, winning both the English Premier League and the FA Cup.
