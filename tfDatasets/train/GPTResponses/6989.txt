Some of the early cultures located in Myanmar include:1. Pyu culture- an ancient Tibeto-Burman speaking people who inhabited central Myanmar from the 2nd century BCE to the 9th century CE.2. Mon culture- an ethnic group that settled in lower Myanmar around the same time as the Pyu, and were known for their maritime skills and Buddhist architecture.3. Bagan culture- a kingdom that rose to power in the 9th century CE and built thousands of Buddhist temples and pagodas throughout central Myanmar.4. Shan culture- an ethnic group of Tai origin that settled in eastern Myanmar and northern Thailand in the 13th century CE.5. Rakhine culture- an ethnic group that inhabited the western coast of Myanmar and were known for their maritime trade and Buddhist art.
