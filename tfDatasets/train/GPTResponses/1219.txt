The Zhejiang model is based on prioritizing and encouraging the growth of small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) through government support and policies. The model aims to create a more inclusive and sustainable economy by fostering entrepreneurship, innovation, and local production.The Zhejiang model emphasizes the importance of a decentralized approach to economic development, with local governments playing a central role in promoting SMEs within their regions. The model also emphasizes the importance of building a strong network of industry clusters, in which groups of related businesses can work together to foster innovation and growth.Overall, the Zhejiang model is designed to create a vibrant and dynamic economy built on a foundation of strong, locally-based SMEs. By encouraging entrepreneurship and innovation, supporting a diverse range of industries and businesses, and promoting sustainable development practices, the model aims to create a more equitable and prosperous society for all.
