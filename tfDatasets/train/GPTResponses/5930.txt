Large corporations can show vertical integration through various ways. Some of these ways include:1. Owning the entire supply chain - from raw materials to finished products - to ensure control over production, quality, and cost.2. Acquiring or investing in companies that provide services or products related to the corporation's main business, so that they can control the entire value chain or make use of economies of scale.3. Developing in-house capabilities in different aspects of the production process or related services, such as marketing, logistics, and distribution.4. Expanding into new markets or product categories through mergers and acquisitions or by building new capabilities in-house.5. Developing proprietary technology or software that improves efficiency or enhances the customer experience throughout the value chain.
