The FBI's program for upgrading its information technology infrastructure is called the “Next Generation Information Technology System” (NGITS) initiative. This initiative aims to modernize the FBI's IT infrastructure, including hardware, software, data centers, networks, and cybersecurity, to enhance the agency's ability to collect, analyze, and share intelligence and evidence in a secure and efficient manner. The NGITS program has a multi-year budget of over $1 billion and involves the collaboration of various government agencies and private sector partners.
