There are several systems that are geared toward mobility:1. Mobile operating systems: These include iOS (Apple), Android (Google), and Windows Mobile (Microsoft) operating systems. They are designed specifically for mobile devices such as smartphones and tablets.2. Mobile device management (MDM) systems: These are used by organizations to manage and secure mobile devices used by their employees. Examples include AirWatch, MobileIron, and BlackBerry UEM.3. Mobile payment systems: These enable payment transactions using mobile devices. Examples include Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Samsung Pay.4. Mobile health (mHealth) systems: These are mobile applications that enable health-related services and information. Examples include MyFitnessPal, Fitbit, and WebMD.5. Mobile marketing systems: These are used for targeted advertising and marketing campaigns on mobile devices. Examples include AdMob, InMobi, and Facebook Ads.6. Mobile learning systems: These are mobile applications and platforms that provide educational content and resources. Examples include Duolingo, Khan Academy, and Udemy.7. Mobile commerce (mCommerce) systems: These enable online shopping and transactions using mobile devices. Examples include Amazon, eBay, and Alibaba.
