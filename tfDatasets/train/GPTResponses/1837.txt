There were several tribes that settled in the Alpine region, including:1. Celts: The Celts were the first known inhabitants of the Alpine region, and they settled in the area around 600 BCE.2. Romans: The Romans conquered much of the Alpine region in the 1st century BCE and established many settlements there.3. Germanic tribes: In the 5th century CE, Germanic tribes such as the Alemanni and the Bavarians began to settle in the Alpine region.4. Slavic tribes: In the 6th century CE, Slavic tribes such as the Carantanians and the Carni began to settle in the Alpine region.5. Lombards: In the 6th century CE, the Lombards migrated from their homeland in Scandinavia and settled in the Alpine region, establishing the Kingdom of the Lombards.
