It is difficult to pinpoint a specific service as there have been numerous instances of shows being removed due to rights issues on various streaming platforms. Some examples include:- In 2020, Friends was removed from Netflix US due to WarnerMedia's decision to move the show to its own streaming service HBO Max.- In 2021, The Office was removed from Netflix US due to NBCUniversal's decision to move the show to its own streaming service Peacock.- In 2017, Disney announced it would be pulling its content from Netflix in preparation for the launch of its own streaming service Disney+.- In 2021, Netflix removed four Chris Lilley shows - Angry Boys, Summer Heights High, We Can Be Heroes, and Jonah from Tonga - due to concerns over the portrayal of race and ethnicity.
