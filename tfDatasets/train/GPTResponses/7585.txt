Triangle Transit, also known as GoTriangle, goes to various locations in the Triangle region of North Carolina, including:- Durham- Raleigh- Chapel Hill- Cary- Morrisville- Garner- Apex- Wake Forest- Holly Springs- Fuquay-Varina- Research Triangle Park (RTP)- RDU AirportIn addition, GoTriangle also offers regional connections to other transit systems, such as GoRaleigh, GoDurham, GoCary, and Chapel Hill Transit, which further expand their service area.
