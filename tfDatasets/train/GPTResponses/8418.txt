Some abstract characters that are represented in Unicode include:1. Various geometric shapes such as squares, circles, triangles, and diamonds.2. Pictograms such as smiley faces, hearts, stars, and arrows.3. Musical notations such as notes, rests, clefs, and time signatures.4. Currency symbols such as the euro, pound sterling, yen, and dollar.5. Mathematical symbols such as infinity, plus-minus, and division.6. Weather symbols such as sun, cloud, and rain.7. Religious symbols such as the cross, Om, and Star of David.These abstract characters are represented in Unicode using a unique code point. For example, the euro symbol is represented by the code point U+20AC, while the infinity symbol is represented by the code point U+221E.
