Hulk Hogan is a retired American professional wrestler, actor, television personality, and entrepreneur. He is best known for his time with the WWE (formerly WWF) and WCW where he became a household name and one of the most recognizable faces in professional wrestling. Hogan has also appeared in numerous movies and television shows, such as Mr. Nanny, Thunder in Paradise, Walker, Texas Ranger, and Miami Vice. He has also launched several successful business ventures, including his own restaurant chain and energy drink line.
