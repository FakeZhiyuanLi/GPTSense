Arsenal have played at Wembley at various times throughout their history, for different competitions and events. Some notable occasions include:- FA Cup finals: Arsenal have played in 20 FA Cup finals at Wembley, most recently in 2020.- League Cup finals: Arsenal have played in 7 League Cup finals at Wembley, most recently in 2018.- Charity Shield/Community Shield: Arsenal have played in numerous Charity Shield/Community Shield matches at Wembley, most recently in 2017.- European Cup Winners' Cup final: Arsenal won the European Cup Winners' Cup final at Wembley in 1994.
