Nouns, adjectives, and verbs are inflected for number in Czech.Nouns:- Singular: denotes one person, animal, thing, or concept (e.g. "dům" - house)- Plural: denotes more than one person, animal, thing, or concept (e.g. "domy" - houses)Adjectives:- Singular: agrees with the noun in gender, case, and number (e.g. "velký dům" - big house)- Plural: agrees with the noun in case and gender, but its form changes for each gender when used with plural nouns (e.g. "velké domy" - big houses (neuter) "velká města" - big cities (feminine) "velcí psi" - big dogs (masculine))
