There are several prizes and awards named after the British writer and philosopher Aldous Huxley. One of them is the Huxley Memorial Medal and Lecture, awarded by the Royal Anthropological Institute for outstanding anthropological research or publication. Another one is the Huxley Prize, awarded annually by Imperial College London to the best undergraduate students in the Department of Life Sciences. The Huxley Prize recognizes academic excellence, innovation, and leadership potential in the fields of life sciences and ecology.
