Two other sub-divisions to describe literature are:1. Genre: This sub-division categorizes literature based on its form or type. The most common genres are poetry, drama, prose (fiction and non-fiction), and literary devices such as satire, allegory, and irony.2. Period: This sub-division categorizes literature based on the historical period it was written in. The most common literary periods are ancient literature, medieval literature, Renaissance literature, Enlightenment literature, Romantic literature, Victorian literature, Modernist literature, and Contemporary literature.
