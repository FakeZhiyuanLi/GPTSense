Yes, there are many other birds that are considered common in the world of poultry, including:- Ducks (such as Pekin ducks and Muscovy ducks)- Geese (such as Embden geese and Toulouse geese)- Turkeys (such as Broad Breasted Bronze and Broad Breasted White)- Guinea fowl- Quail (such as Coturnix quail and Bobwhite quail)- Pheasants (such as Ring-necked pheasants and Golden pheasants)- Emus and ostriches (although less common than others on this list)
