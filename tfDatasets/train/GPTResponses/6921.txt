Sir Winston Churchill held many positions throughout his long career, including:1. Prime Minister of the United Kingdom (1940–1945, 1951–1955)2. Home Secretary of the United Kingdom (1910–1911)3. First Lord of the Admiralty (1911–1915, 1939–1940)4. Secretary of State for War and Air (1919–1921)5. Chancellor of the Exchequer (1924–1929)6. Leader of the Opposition (1929–1935)7. Minister of Defence (1940–1945)8. Minister of Defence and Deputy Prime Minister (1951–1955)Additionally, Churchill held various other government positions and military posts throughout his career.
