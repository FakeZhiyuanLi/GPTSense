Some notable landmarks and attractions within the precincts of Westminster Abbey include:1. Houses of Parliament - home to the British government and the famous Big Ben clock tower.2. Westminster Palace Gardens - a beautiful public park with views of the River Thames.3. Jewel Tower - a medieval tower that once housed the treasures of the Crown.4. Churchill War Rooms - the underground bunker from which Winston Churchill directed Britain's WWII efforts.5. St. Margaret's Church - a historic church adjacent to Westminster Abbey.6. Buckingham Palace - the official residence of the British monarchy.7. Victoria Tower Gardens - a riverside park with views of the Palace of Westminster and the London Eye.
