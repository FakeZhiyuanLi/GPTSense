1. Golden Horde: A confederation of Mongol and Turkic tribes that ruled parts of Russia, Ukraine, Kazakhstan, and Uzbekistan in the 13th and 14th centuries.2. Timurid Empire: A confederation of Mongol, Turkic, and Persian tribes that ruled an empire in Central Asia, Iran, and parts of India in the 14th and 15th centuries.3. Kazakh Khanate: A confederation of Turkic tribes that ruled Kazakhstan and neighboring regions in the 15th-18th centuries.4. Mughal Empire: A confederation of Mongol, Turkic, and Persian tribes that ruled over the Indian subcontinent from the 16th to the 19th century.5. Ottoman Empire: A confederation of Turkic and Arabic tribes that ruled over much of southeastern Europe, western Asia, and North Africa from the 14th to the 20th century.6. Safavid Empire: A confederation of Turkic and Persian tribes that ruled over Persia and neighboring regions from the 16th to the 18th century.7. Crimean Khanate: A confederation of Turkic tribes that ruled over Crimea and parts of Ukraine from the 15th to the 18th century.
