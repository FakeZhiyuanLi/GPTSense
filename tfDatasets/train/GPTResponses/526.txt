During the Middle Ages, art focused on religious themes, and the most popular scenes depicted in art were:1. The Crucifixion of Jesus Christ2. The Virgin Mary with the Christ Child3. Scenes from the life of Jesus Christ, such as the Nativity, the Last Supper, and the Resurrection4. The Judgment Day5. Saints and their miracles6. Biblical stories, such as David and Goliath, Adam and Eve, and Noah's Ark7. The Apocalypse and the Four Horsemen8. The Seven Deadly Sins and the Seven Virtues9. Portraits of rulers and nobility10. Decorative patterns and motifs, such as animals, plants, and geometric shapes.
