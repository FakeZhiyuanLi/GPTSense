The Eastern Orthodox Church celebrates twelve great feasts:1. The Nativity of the Theotokos (September 8)2. The Exaltation of the Cross (September 14)3. The Presentation of the Theotokos (November 21)4. The Nativity of Christ (December 25)5. The Theophany (January 6)6. The Presentation of Christ in the Temple (February 2)7. The Annunciation (March 25)8. Palm Sunday9. Easter10. Ascension11. Pentecost12. The Transfiguration (August 6)
