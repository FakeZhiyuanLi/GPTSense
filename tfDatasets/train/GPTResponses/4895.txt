The concept of identity helps map and define people's experiences of self in society. Identity refers to how individuals see themselves and how they are perceived by others, based on various factors such as age, ethnicity, gender, culture, and social status. It helps individuals understand the roles they play in society and how they fit into different social structures. Identity is constantly evolving and can be influenced by personal experiences, social interactions, and cultural norms. It is a complex and multifaceted concept that helps shape one's sense of self and their place in the world.
