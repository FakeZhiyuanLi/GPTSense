Boeing has several other manufacturing facilities for airplanes. Some of the notable locations include:- Everett, Washington: The largest manufacturing facility of Boeing, where wide-body aircraft such as the 747, 767, 777, and 787 are built.- North Charleston, South Carolina: The second-largest manufacturing site of Boeing, where the 787 Dreamliner is assembled.- St. Louis, Missouri: The home of defense and space programs of Boeing, where military aircraft such as the F/A-18 Super Hornet, EA-18G Growler, and F-15 Eagle are produced.- Philadelphia, Pennsylvania: The site for the production of rotorcraft such as the H-47 Chinook and V-22 Osprey.- Huntsville, Alabama: The center for space exploration and defense programs of Boeing, where the SLS rocket and other advanced technologies are developed and produced.
