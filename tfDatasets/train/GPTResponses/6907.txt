Ric Flair is a professional wrestler who has accomplished a great deal in his career. Some of his notable accomplishments include:- Winning 16 World Championships (recognized by the major promotions he competed in at the time, including the NWA, WCW, and WWE)- Being inducted into the WWE Hall of Fame in 2008- Being named the greatest professional wrestler of all time by WWE, Pro Wrestling Illustrated, and many other publications- Being known for his charismatic personality and flamboyant attire, which helped to popularize the "Nature Boy" persona in wrestling- Being a key figure in the rise of the "Four Horsemen," a popular wrestling stable in the 1980s and 1990s.
