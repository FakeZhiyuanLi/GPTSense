The 150th anniversary of the publication of "On the Origin of Species" was celebrated with various events around the world. In the UK, the Natural History Museum hosted a major exhibition entitled "Darwin's Big Idea", which explored Darwin's life, research and legacy. The Royal Society organized a series of lectures and discussions on Darwin's contributions to science. The American Museum of Natural History in New York City held a symposium entitled "Darwin's Legacy: Evolution's Impact on Science and Culture". The University of Cambridge also celebrated the anniversary by organizing a series of talks, exhibitions and public events. Several books were also published to commemorate the occasion, including "Charles Darwin: A Celebration of His Life and Legacy" and "Darwin: The Indelible Stamp".
