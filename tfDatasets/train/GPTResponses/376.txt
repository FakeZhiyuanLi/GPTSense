Middle Iron Age remnants have been found in various parts of the world, including Europe, Africa, and Asia. Some notable locations include:1. Europe: Remnants of Middle Iron Age settlements and artifacts have been found in various parts of Europe, including central and eastern Europe, Scandinavia, and the British Isles.2. Africa: Middle Iron Age cultures flourished in various parts of Africa, including the Sudanic regions of West Africa, the Great Lakes region of East Africa, and southern Africa.3. Asia: The Middle Iron Age period in Asia is generally associated with the emergence of the Iron Age in the Indian subcontinent, as well as the Zhou dynasty in China. Remnants of Middle Iron Age cultures have also been found in the Middle East.Some notable Middle Iron Age cultures include the Hallstatt culture in Europe, the Nok culture in Africa, and the Vedic culture in India.
