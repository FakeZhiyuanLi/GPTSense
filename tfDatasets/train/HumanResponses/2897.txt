course objective this course is designed to help students understand that high school is different from middle school and that there are certain expectations and personal responsibilities that are required to be a successful student and to graduate. the main concepts to be covered in this course focus on goal setting, personal actions, study and critical thinking skills, organization, and basic computer applications. this course is to serve a three-fold function: 1) to help students develop a sense of their own ability to succeed; 2) to give them a set of tools to empower then to be successful in accomplishing their personal and academic goals; 3) to give them practice in using these tools.
