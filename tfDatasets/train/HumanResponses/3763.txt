how could this chrysanthemum or virgin carnation grow up to be a potato the shade of crumbling drought-raped dirt? pressing down out swelling bucking against her roots outstretched arms dangle deadly ivy vines in her dark fairytale forest she sees past their poison. yellow-brick-road shimmering like voluptuous asphalt on i-12 flanked with speeding pines and running dead smash into that heaven on the horizon in her freshly rewound dream the city basks in the humid sunset she is a misunderstood harlot of opportunities and she rolls on the river a rotting red stick
