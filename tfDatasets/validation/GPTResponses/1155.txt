The philosophical term that corresponds to the type of interpretation in ta'wil is hermeneutics. Hermeneutics is the theory and practice of interpretation, especially the interpretation of texts. Ta'wil is a method of interpretation used in Islamic philosophy and theology to interpret scripture and other texts. It involves going beyond the literal meaning of the text to arrive at a deeper understanding of its meaning and significance. Hermeneutics is a broader term that encompasses various forms of interpretation and is applied in many different fields, including literature, law, and social sciences.
