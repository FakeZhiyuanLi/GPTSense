Some factors that helped to advance the Greek way of life beyond its borders include:1. Trade: The Greeks were skilled traders and their goods, ideas, and culture spread through trade routes.2. Colonization: The Greeks established colonies in other parts of the Mediterranean, spreading Greek culture and language.3. Alexander the Great: The conquests of Alexander the Great spread Greek culture and ideas throughout his empire.4. Philosophy: Greek philosophers like Plato, Aristotle, and Socrates had a significant impact on Western thought and influenced thinkers throughout history.5. Art and Architecture: Greek art and architecture, such as the Parthenon in Athens, became a model for future generations and were studied and imitated by other cultures.6. Olympic Games: The ancient Olympic Games, which originated in Greece, brought together athletes and spectators from different parts of the world, increasing awareness of Greek culture and ideas.
