The Greeks had a significant influence on the artistry of the world. Their style and techniques in art were renowned and admired throughout the world.Greek artists created sculptures and paintings that portrayed their ideals of beauty and perfection. They used techniques such as realism, three-dimensional representation, and proportional symmetry, which continue to be important in modern art.Their art works also influenced the Roman, Renaissance, and Baroque periods. The Renaissance artists studied Greek art, which influenced their styles greatly.Greek art has been widely revered by artists and art enthusiasts worldwide, making it a significant contributor to the global artistry canon.
