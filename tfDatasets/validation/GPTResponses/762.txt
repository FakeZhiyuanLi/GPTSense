Educational psychologists are professionals who specialize in studying and understanding human learning and development. They also apply this knowledge to identify and solve problems related to education and learning. Some of the individuals who may be identified as educational psychologists include:1. School psychologists2. Educational consultants3. Academic support specialists4. Education researchers5. School counselors6. Learning disabilities specialists7. Child psychologists8. School administrators and leaders with a focus on student and staff learning and development9. Behavioral specialists or interventionists with a focus on educational contexts.
