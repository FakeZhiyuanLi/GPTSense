1) Lisbon Metropolitan Area: Lisbon, the capital city of Portugal, is the economic and financial center of the country. Many industries, businesses and financial institutions have their headquarters in Lisbon and its surrounding areas. This region is also home to many research and technology centers, universities, and international companies.2) Porto Metropolitan Area: Porto is the second largest city in Portugal and is known for its historic center and port wine production. This region is a hub for many industries including textiles, food processing, chemical production, and machinery manufacturing. Many international companies also have their headquarters or subsidiaries in Porto. Additionally, the region is a significant financial center, with many banks and financial institutions headquartered or operating there.
