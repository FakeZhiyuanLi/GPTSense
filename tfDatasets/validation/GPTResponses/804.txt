The music in Afro Samurai is a blend of hip-hop, R&B, rock, and traditional Japanese instruments. The soundtrack was composed by RZA, a member of the Wu-Tang Clan, and features collaborations with various other artists, including Ghostface Killah, Talib Kweli, and Kool G Rap. The music was widely praised for its unique blend of Western and Eastern styles, which complimented the show's hybrid aesthetic of samurai culture and futuristic technology.
