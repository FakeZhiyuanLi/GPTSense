The scarab beetle is considered symbolic in the ancient world. It was a symbol of new life and rebirth in ancient Egyptian and Greek cultures. In ancient Egypt, the scarab beetle was also associated with the god Khepri, who represented the rising sun and the cyclical nature of life.
