Zinc pyrithione is used in various consumer products and industrial applications, including:1. Antidandruff shampoos and hair conditioners to prevent or treat flaking and itching of the scalp.2. Skin care products such as soaps, face washes, and lotions to kill bacteria, fungi and prevent acne.3. Paints to inhibit the growth of mildew and other fungi on surfaces.4. Adhesives and sealants to prevent microbial growth.5. Plastics and rubber as a biocide to prevent microbial deterioration.6. Textiles and fabrics to prevent odor and bacterial growth.7. Cooling towers and water treatment systems to prevent bacterial growth and biofilm formation.It is also used in the treatment of psoriasis and eczema.
