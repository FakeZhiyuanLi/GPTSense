The founders of progressive education were John Dewey, Maria Montessori, and Friedrich Froebel. John Dewey is often considered the father of progressive education and his ideas heavily influenced the movement. Maria Montessori pioneered the Montessori Method of education, which emphasizes self-directed learning and hands-on, experiential learning. Friedrich Froebel was a German educational theorist who introduced the concept of kindergarten, which focused on play and exploration as a method of learning.
