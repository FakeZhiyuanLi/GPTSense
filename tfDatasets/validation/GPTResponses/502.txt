Werner von Siemens himself utilized his own ideas to build a successful business. He co-founded Siemens & Halske, now known as Siemens AG, which is a multinational conglomerate company that focuses on electronics, automation, and digitization. Over the years, Siemens has become one of the largest engineering companies in the world with a presence in over 200 countries. The company continues to innovate and evolve, utilizing von Siemens' original ideas as a foundation for its success.
