1. Military Strength: Historically, military strength has been an important factor in determining a great power's status. The ability to project military power and influence events in other regions can establish a country as a great power.2. Economic Power: Economic power, such as having a large GDP or being a major exporter, is another factor that can determine a country's great power status. Economic prowess allows countries to exert influence by offering economic incentives or placing economic sanctions on other nations.3. Diplomatic Influence: A country's diplomatic influence in global institutions, such as the United Nations, can enhance its status as a great power. Strong alliances with other nations can help a country achieve diplomatic success and promote its interests on the global stage.
