The Greeks' success of establishment can be attributed to several factors such as:1. Systematic colonization: The Greeks established colonies throughout the Mediterranean, Black Sea, and other regions. These colonies helped spread Greek culture, language, and ideas, which contributed to the Greek's success.2. Military prowess: The Greeks had a strong military and were skilled in warfare. They used their military power to defend their territory and expand their influence.3. Political structure: The Greeks had a system of city-states that promoted individualism and democracy. This allowed for the development of innovative ideas and competition between states.4. Cultural achievements: The Greeks made significant contributions to art, science, mathematics, philosophy, and literature. These cultural achievements contributed to the Greek's success and legacy.5. Trade and commerce: The Greeks were skilled traders and established extensive trade networks. This allowed for the exchange of goods and ideas, which contributed to the Greek's economic and cultural prosperity.
