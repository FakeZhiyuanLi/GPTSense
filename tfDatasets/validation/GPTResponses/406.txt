There are many monuments dedicated to the victims of war and violence around the world. Some of the most well-known include:- The Vietnam Veterans Memorial in Washington D.C., USA- The Yad Vashem Holocaust Memorial in Jerusalem, Israel- The Hiroshima Peace Memorial in Hiroshima, Japan- The Memorial to the Murdered Jews of Europe in Berlin, Germany- The Memorial to the Unknown Soldier in many countries, including the USA, UK, and France.There are many others as well, each with their own unique name and design.
