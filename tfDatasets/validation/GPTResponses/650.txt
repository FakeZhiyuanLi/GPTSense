Tertiary education includes education and training that is provided after graduation from secondary education, which typically includes high schools, colleges or vocational schools. Tertiary education includes universities, colleges, institutes of technology, and vocational schools, where individuals can pursue higher-level education and training programs. Tertiary education programs often offer undergraduate, graduate, and postgraduate degree programs, vocational training, professional development, and research opportunities. Examples of tertiary education programs include bachelor's and master's degree programs, doctoral programs, vocational programs, professional development courses, and continuing education programs.
