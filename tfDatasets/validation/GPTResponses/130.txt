The movie "Munich" is a 2005 historical drama/thriller film directed by Steven Spielberg. It tells the story of the aftermath of the 1972 Olympics in Munich, Germany, where Palestinian terrorists from the Black September group took the Israeli Olympic team hostage and killed 11 of its members. The movie follows a group of Israeli agents who are sent to track down and kill those responsible for the attack. The film explores themes such as revenge, justice, and the cost of violence, and draws attention to the complex nature of the Israeli-Palestinian conflict.
