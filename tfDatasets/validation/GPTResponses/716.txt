There are many stations that do not identify themselves by their call letters, but instead use a branding or slogan. For example, some popular stations in the US include:- KIIS-FM (Los Angeles, CA) - "102.7 KIIS FM"- Z100 (New York, NY) - "New York's #1 Hit Music Station"- HOT 97 (New York, NY) - "Hot 97"- B96 (Chicago, IL) - "Chicago's #1 Hit Music Station"- Power 106 (Los Angeles, CA) - "Power 106"
