During this time, the important heritage of art from Greece that was celebrated was the Classical Period of ancient Greek art. This period, which lasted from the 5th century BC to the 4th century BC, is known for its monumental architecture, sculpture, and pottery. The Parthenon, a temple dedicated to the goddess Athena in Athens, is one of the most famous examples of classical Greek architecture. The sculpture of this period featured idealized human forms, and is characterized by its attention to detail, balance, and proportion. Pottery from this period is also known for its beauty and sophistication, and it featured intricate designs and intricate ornamentation.
