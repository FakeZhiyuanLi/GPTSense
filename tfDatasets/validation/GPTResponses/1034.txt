Eschatology is a general topic in theology and philosophy that deals with the study of the end times, the ultimate fate of humanity, and the end of the world. It explores questions about the nature of God, humanity, and the universe, and discusses topics such as the judgment day, eternal life, the final resurrection, and the afterlife. Eschatology is concerned with exploring the ultimate meaning and purpose of life and existence, and how our actions and decisions in this life impact our eternal destiny.
