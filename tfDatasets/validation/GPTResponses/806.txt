Spiral screws are used for a variety of applications including:1. Fastening materials together: Spiral screws are commonly used to hold two or more pieces of material together, such as in construction or woodworking.2. Transporting materials: Spiral screws are often used in conveyor systems to move materials from one place to another.3. Pumping liquids and gases: Spiral screws can be used in pumps and compressors to move liquids and gases.4. Mixing materials: Spiral screws can be used in mixing systems to combine materials in precise proportions.5. Extracting solids and liquids: Spiral screws can be used in machinery used for separating solids and liquids, such as in wastewater treatment plants.
