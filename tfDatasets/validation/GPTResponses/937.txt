The four recognized levels of testing software are:1. Unit Testing - This involves testing individual components or modules of the software to ensure they work as intended.2. Integration Testing - This involves testing the interactions between different modules or components of the software to ensure they work together without issues.3. System Testing - This involves testing the entire system as a whole to ensure it meets the specified requirements and works as expected.4. Acceptance Testing - This involves testing the software against the user's requirements and expectations to ensure it is ready to be released and used by the end-users.
