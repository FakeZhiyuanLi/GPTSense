Many things have been crossed for war and commerce throughout history. Some examples include:- Borders: Countries and nations have crossed borders to wage wars and expand their territories. Borders have also been crossed to trade goods and resources.- Oceans: Ships and naval forces have crossed oceans to engage in war and to establish trade routes with other countries and continents.- Indigenous cultures: Throughout history, indigenous cultures have been exploited and displaced for the sake of acquiring resources for trade and expansion.- Resources: Wars have been fought over coveted resources such as oil, gold, and diamonds. These same resources have been traded and used to fuel economic growth and prosperity.- Human lives: Wars and conflicts have caused the loss of countless human lives. On the other hand, commerce and trade have led to the exploitation and mistreatment of workers in pursuit of profits.Overall, the intersection of war and commerce has had both devastating and transformative effects on human societies throughout history.
