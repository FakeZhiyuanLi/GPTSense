Various species have dominated the seas at different times throughout history. Some of the most dominant species include:1. Marine reptiles such as ichthyosaurs and plesiosaurs during the Mesozoic Era (252 to 66 million years ago)2. Sharks, such as megalodon, during the Cenozoic Era (66 million years ago to present)3. Baleen whales, such as blue whales, in modern times (20 million years ago to present)4. Dinoflagellates, a type of plankton, which make up a large portion of the ocean's biomass and provide the base of the food chain.
