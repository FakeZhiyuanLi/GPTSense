There are several factors that can give glass a yellow color, including:1. Iron oxide: If the glass contains iron as an impurity, it can cause a yellowish-brown tint.2. Age and exposure to sunlight: Over time, glass can become yellow due to exposure to UV light and other environmental factors.3. Manufacturing process: The way the glass is made, heated and cooled can also affect its color.4. Composition of raw materials: The type and quality of raw materials used in making the glass can also influence its color.5. Thickness of the glass: Thicker glass can appear more yellow than thinner glass, particularly if it contains impurities.
