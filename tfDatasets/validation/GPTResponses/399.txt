There were a few rebellions that happened in 1160:1. Heiji Rebellion: This was a civil war fought between the Taira and Minamoto clans in Japan. It began on January 19, 1160, and lasted for over four months. The Taira clan emerged victorious.2. Norman Revolt of 1160: This was a rebellion in Normandy against the rule of King Henry II of England. It was led by the Earl of Leicester and was eventually crushed by the King's forces.3. Revolt of the baronage: This was a rebellion by the barons of Ghent against the Count of Flanders, Philip I. It began in 1159 and continued into 1160 before being put down by the Count's forces.
