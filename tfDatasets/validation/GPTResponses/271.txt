the evolution process?Homo sapiens, or modern humans, developed various attributes during the course of their evolution process. Some of these attributes include:1. Bipedalism - the ability to walk on two feet2. Large brain capacity and cognitive abilities such as language and problem-solving3. Opposable thumbs for tools and increased dexterity4. Increased stamina and endurance, allowing for longer periods of activity5. Reduced gut size and improved digestive system, allowing for a more varied diet6. Enhanced social skills and capacity for cooperation and teamwork7. Development of art, music, and culture, indicating increased abstract thinking and creative expression8. Increased lifespan and a reduction in infant mortality rates due to improved medical knowledge and technology.
