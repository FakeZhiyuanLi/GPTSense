During the Han Dynasty, the government implemented a number of agricultural reforms to help smaller landowners. These included:1. Land redistribution: The government redistributed land to break up large estates and give smaller farmers access to land.2. Tax reductions: The Han government reduced taxes on small landowners to ease their financial burden.3. Irrigation projects: The government built irrigation systems to help farmers irrigate their fields during dry seasons.4. Technical assistance: The government provided farming techniques to smaller landowners to help them increase their crop yield.5. Legal protection: The government passed laws to protect small landowners from encroachment by larger estates or powerful individuals.Overall, these initiatives aimed to improve the livelihoods of smaller landowners, who made up a significant portion of the Han population.
