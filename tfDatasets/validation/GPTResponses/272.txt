There are many parts of the world that became urbanized in the 20th century. Some examples include:1. Asia: Many Asian countries such as China, India, and Bangladesh experienced rapid urbanization in the 20th century. This was due to various factors such as population growth, industrialization, and rural-to-urban migration.2. Latin America: Urbanization also accelerated in Latin America during the 20th century. Countries such as Brazil, Mexico, and Argentina experienced rapid population growth and urbanization due to urban job opportunities and migration from rural areas.3. Africa: African countries also experienced urbanization in the 20th century, although to a lesser extent than other continents. South Africa, Nigeria, and Egypt are some of the countries that experienced urbanization due to industrialization and urban job opportunities.Overall, urbanization was a global phenomenon in the 20th century, and many parts of the world experienced rapid urban growth during this period.
