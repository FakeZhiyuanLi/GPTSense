Many photographers are known for photographing night scenes. Some of the most famous include:1. Brassai: This Hungarian-French photographer captured stunning night photos of Paris in the 1930s.2. Weegee: This New York City photographer documented crime scenes and nightlife in the 1940s and 50s.3. Todd Hido: This contemporary American photographer captures haunting and mysterious night scenes of suburban neighborhoods.4. Michael Kenna: This British photographer creates serene and minimalist night photos of landscapes, architecture, and industrial scenes.5. Jeff Wall: This Canadian artist creates large-scale, cinematic photographs that often depict scenes from city life at night.
