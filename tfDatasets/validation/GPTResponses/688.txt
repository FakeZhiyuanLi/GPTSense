The times that are generally reserved for local newscasts vary depending on the region and the specific channel. However, the most common times for local newscasts are in the early morning (between 5:00 a.m. and 7:00 a.m.), in the evening (between 5:00 p.m. and 7:00 p.m.), and at night (between 10:00 p.m. and 11:00 p.m.). Some channels may also have midday newscasts (between 11:00 a.m. and 12:00 p.m.).
