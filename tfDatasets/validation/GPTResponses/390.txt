The "movie brats" is a term used to refer to a group of American filmmakers who emerged in the 1970s and revolutionized Hollywood filmmaking. Besides Spielberg, the key members of the group include:1. George Lucas - creator of the Star Wars franchise and co-founder of Industrial Light and Magic2. Francis Ford Coppola - director of The Godfather trilogy3. Martin Scorsese - director of Taxi Driver and Goodfellas4. Brian De Palma - director of Carrie and Scarface5. John Milius - writer and director of Conan the Barbarian and Red Dawn.
