There have been many individuals who have translated written works into Chinese throughout history. Some famous examples include:- Yan Fu, who translated works by French social and political thinkers such as Rousseau, Montesquieu, and Spencer in the late 19th century- Lin Yutang, who translated works by Shakespeare, Thoreau, and Emerson into Chinese in the early 20th century- Dai Wangshu, who translated poetry by T.S. Eliot, W.H. Auden, and Dylan Thomas into Chinese in the mid-20th century- Xu Zhimo, who translated works by Keats, Shelley, and Byron into Chinese in the early 20th century- Qian Zhongshu, who translated works by Goethe, Joyce, and Kafka into Chinese in the mid-20th century.
