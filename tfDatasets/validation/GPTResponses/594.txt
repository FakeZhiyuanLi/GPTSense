There were many areas being explored by sea faring vessels throughout history. Some examples include:1. Viking Exploration: The Vikings explored much of the North Atlantic, reaching as far as North America in the 11th century2. Chinese Exploration: In the early 1400s, Chinese explorer Zheng He led expeditions to Southeast Asia, India, the Middle East, and East Africa.3. European Exploration: In the 15th and 16th centuries, European explorers such as Christopher Columbus, Vasco da Gama, Ferdinand Magellan, and Francis Drake explored the Americas, Africa, Asia, and Australia.4. Polynesian Exploration: The Polynesians were accomplished navigators who explored and settled islands throughout the Pacific Ocean. They are believed to have arrived in Hawaii around 1000 AD.The exact dates of arrival for these explorers varies depending on the specific expedition and the area being explored.
