Overuse of zinc can cause several side effects such as:1. Nausea and vomiting2. Diarrhea3. Stomach cramps4. Headaches5. Loss of appetite6. Metallic taste in the mouth7. Liver damage8. Copper deficiency9. Anemia10. Lowered immune system function11. Kidney problemsOveruse of zinc supplements can also interfere with the absorption of other important minerals like iron and copper, which can lead to their deficiencies. It is important to talk to a healthcare provider before taking zinc supplements, especially in high doses or for prolonged periods of time.
