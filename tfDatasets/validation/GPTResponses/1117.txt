There were several individuals and groups who created Quranic manuscripts before the 19th century. Some of the most notable include:1. The scribes of the Prophet Muhammad and his companions who wrote down the Quran during the 7th century.2. The Umayyad caliphs who commissioned the standardization of the Quranic text in the 8th century.3. The Abbasid caliphs who sponsored the production of ornate and illuminated Quranic manuscripts from the 9th to the 13th century.4. The Persian calligrapher and manuscript artist Abu Ali Muhammad ibn Ali ibn Muqlah who produced several highly regarded Quranic manuscripts during the 10th century.5. The Ottoman sultans who patronized the creation of large-scale illuminated Quranic manuscripts during the 15th to 17th centuries.
