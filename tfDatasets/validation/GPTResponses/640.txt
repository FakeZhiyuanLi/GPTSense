The purpose of secondary education is to prepare students for higher education or to enter the workforce. It helps students develop knowledge, skills, and attitudes that will enable them to be successful in their future endeavors. It provides students with a foundation in core academic subjects such as mathematics, science, and language arts and offers opportunities to explore various fields and personal interests through elective courses. Secondary education also helps students develop critical thinking abilities, social skills, and personal responsibility, which are crucial for success both in college and in the workforce.
