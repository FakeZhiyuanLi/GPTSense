Universities are typically made up of various faculties, schools, departments, and centers/institutes, which offer programs in different fields, such as arts and humanities, social sciences, sciences, engineering, business, law, medicine, etc. These institutions may have their own administrative and academic structures, including staff, professors, researchers, and students who engage in teaching, research, and other forms of academic activities. Universities may also have support units/services, such as libraries, IT, student affairs, career services, and athletics, among others.
