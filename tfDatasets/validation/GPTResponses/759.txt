Education psychology refers to the scientific study of human learning and development within educational settings. This field of psychology explores the cognitive, social, and emotional processes that influence learning, teaching, and academic achievement. Education psychologists study a variety of topics, such as student motivation, learning and memory, instructional methods, classroom management, student assessment, and the impact of educational policies and practices on student outcomes. The ultimate goal of education psychology is to inform and improve teaching and learning practices to enhance student success and well-being.
