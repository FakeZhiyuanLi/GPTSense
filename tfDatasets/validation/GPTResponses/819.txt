There are several indicators that can indicate the arrival of the elevator at a new floor:1. The elevator doors will open and close.2. The floor number will be displayed on an electronic display near the elevator doors.3. A chime or beep may sound to indicate the arrival.4. The elevator may come to a stop and the movement may suddenly become smoother as the elevator levels out with the new floor.5. The light above the elevator doors may change to indicate the new floor level.
