Modern Portuguese poetry is rooted in various styles, including:1. Symbolism: This movement emphasized the use of symbols and metaphors to express emotions and ideas.2. Futurism: This avant-garde movement celebrated technology, industry, and innovation.3. Surrealism: This movement explored the subconscious mind and the irrational.4. Neorealism: This movement focused on the reality of everyday life, often with social and political commentary.5. Experimentalism: This style challenged traditional forms and structures, often mixing genres and incorporating visual elements.6. Minimalism: This style focused on simplicity and sparse language.7. Postmodernism: This movement rejected traditional narratives and embraced fragmentation, intertextuality, and irony.
